S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
RECOGNIZING "REACH OUT AND READ RHODE ISLAND DAY" ON MARCH 3, 2026 IN
THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Caldwell, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Chippendale,
Kennedy, Ajello, Shallcross Smith, Fellela, Diaz, and Fogarty
Date Introduced: March 03, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, Reach Out and Read Rhode Island is a local, nonprofit organization
2 dedicated to building a world where every child is read to every day by supporting families with
3 young children through the power of shared reading and early literacy; and
4 WHEREAS, The organization partners with pediatricians and family medicine providers
5 across Rhode Island to encourage families to read aloud every day starting at birth as a way to
6 build early literacy skills, strengthen family bonds and empower parents to be their child's first
7 and most important teacher; and
8 WHEREAS, Families participating in the Reach Out and Read RI program receive
9 developmentally appropriate, new books at no cost during well-child visits from birth through age
10 five, helping to build home libraries and spark early developmental, language, and emotional
11 skills; and
12 WHEREAS, Reading aloud with young children not only promotes language acquisition
13 and cognitive development but also strengthens parent-child bonds and supports healthier brain
14 growth during the critical early years of life; and
15 WHEREAS, Reach Out and Read RI has served Rhode Island since 1999, currently
16 partnering with nearly 70 medical sites statewide and a network of over 400 medical clinicians,
17 providing books and literacy guidance at more than 100,000 well-child visits last year alone and
18 serving 50,000 children; and
19 WHEREAS, Recognizing and celebrating Reach Out and Read RI Day on March 3 raises

1 public awareness of the importance of early childhood literacy, encourages community support
2 for family reading, and honors the dedicated pediatric partners and volunteers who serve Rhode
3 Island’s children; now, therefore be it
4 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
5 commemorates March 3, 2026, as "Reach Out and Read Rhode Island Day" in the State of Rhode
6 Island in recognition of the vital role that early literacy, family reading, and community
7 engagement play in the healthy development and lifelong success of Rhode Island’s youngest
8 residents; and be it further
9 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
10 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Aimee Falso, Executive Director of Reach Out
11 and Read RI.
