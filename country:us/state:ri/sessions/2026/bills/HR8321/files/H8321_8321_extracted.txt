S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
HONORING AND CONGRATULATING BRIANNA VEGA, MISS RHODE ISLAND USA,
FOR HER OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENTS, LEADERSHIP, AND PROUD
REPRESENTATION OF THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Diaz, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Chippendale, Paplauskas,
Stewart, Cruz, Alzate, Kennedy, and Azzinaro
Date Introduced: March 19, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, Brianna Vega was born and raised in the City of Providence, Rhode Island,
2 and is the proud daughter of Dominican immigrant parents and the granddaughter of women who
3 came to the United States in search of greater opportunities for their families; and
4 WHEREAS, The courage, sacrifice, and perseverance of her family instilled in her a deep
5 pride in her Dominican heritage and a lifelong commitment to uplifting her community; and
6 WHEREAS, Ms. Vega graduated from Classical High School in 2014 and continued her
7 education at the University of Rhode Island, where she earned a degree in Nursing; and
8 WHEREAS, She began her career as a Registered Nurse serving the community in which
9 she was raised, the City of Providence, and now specializes in Obstetrics and Gynecology, where
10 she is dedicated to supporting and advocating for women throughout their healthcare journeys;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, In addition to her professional work in health care, Brianna Vega is a
13 certified personal trainer who believes in creating safe spaces where individuals feel empowered
14 to pursue healthy lifestyles; and
15 WHEREAS, Ms. Vega is also committed to mentorship and youth empowerment as a
16 volunteer “Big” with Big Brothers Big Sisters of Rhode Island, where she works to inspire young
17 people and reinforce the importance of believing in their potential; and
18 WHEREAS, Brianna Vega currently serves as the reigning Miss Rhode Island USA,
19 proudly representing the Ocean State at the Miss USA competition, where she was named a Top

1 20 semifinalist, marking the first placement for Rhode Island in more than a decade; and
2 WHEREAS, Ms. Vega was also one of only three Dominican women to walk the Miss
3 USA stage this year, representing both Rhode Island and the Dominican-American community
4 with immense pride and distinction; now, therefore be it
5 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
6 honors and congratulates Brianna Vega, Miss Rhode Island USA, for her achievements,
7 leadership, and commitment to service, and proudly recognizes her as an inspiring role model for
8 young women and communities throughout Rhode Island; and be it further
9 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
10 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Brianna Vega, Miss Rhode Island USA.
