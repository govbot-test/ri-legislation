S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- TEACHERS' RETIREMENT
Introduced By: Senator V. Susan Sosnowski
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: Senate Labor & Gaming
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 16-16 of the General Laws entitled "Teachers’ Retirement [See Title
2 16 Chapter 97 — The Rhode Island Board of Education Act]" is hereby amended by adding thereto
3 the following section:
4 16-16-44. Member employment after retirement.
5 (a) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, any retired member of the
6 employees’ retirement system of Rhode Island, under titles 16, 36, or 45, may be employed in a
7 full-time or part-time position, within the employees’ retirement system of Rhode Island, without
8 any forfeiture of or reduction in, their retirement benefits and allowances the member is receiving,
9 or may receive, provided the retired member:
10 (i) Resides in Rhode Island for two hundred seventy (270) days in a calendar year; and
11 (ii) Has an annual gross income from all sources that is less than fifty-five thousand dollars
12 ($55,000).
13 (b) Notice of the reemployment shall be sent monthly to the state retirement board by the
14 employer and by the retired member.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- TEACHERS' RETIREMENT
***
1 This act would allow retired teachers, state and municipal workers to be employed without
2 the loss of any retirement benefits, as long as they reside in Rhode Island at least two hundred
3 seventy (270) days and have an annual income from all sources that is less than fifty-five thousand
4 dollars ($55,000).
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
