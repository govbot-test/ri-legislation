S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
CONGRATULATING MIRANDA MARIE MUSUMECI ON BEING NAMED MISS
IRELAND IN THE WEST WARWICK ST. PATRICK'S DAY PARADE 2026
Introduced By: Senators Raptakis, Britto, Felag, Murray, Rogers, Vargas, Acosta,
Appollonio, LaMountain, and Mack
Date Introduced: March 12, 2026
Referred To: Recommended for Immediate Consideration
1 WHEREAS, The State of Rhode Island takes great pride in celebrating the rich Irish
2 heritage and traditions that have contributed to the cultural fabric of our state and nation; and
3 WHEREAS, St. Patrick’s Day is a time-honored occasion that brings communities
4 together in joyous celebration, and the annual St. Patrick’s Day Parade serves as a meaningful
5 tribute to Irish culture, history, and contributions to society; and
6 WHEREAS, Miranda Marie Musumeci has been selected as Miss Ireland for the West
7 Warwick St. Patrick’s Day Parade 2026, an honor that recognizes her outstanding character,
8 leadership, and embodiment of Irish heritage and pride; and
9 WHEREAS, Miranda Marie Musumeci's selection as Miss Ireland reflects her dedication
10 to community service, cultural appreciation, and her role as a positive representative of Irish-
11 American traditions; and
12 WHEREAS, Her participation in the parade as Miss Ireland is a testament to her
13 commitment to celebrating and preserving the values and spirit of the Irish community in Rhode
14 Island; and
15 WHEREAS, Miranda is currently a student at both Coventry High School and the
16 Community College of Rhode Island, where she is involved in a variety of extracurricular
17 activities including the Naval Junior Reserve Officer Training Corps (NJROTC) program, where
18 she is a member of the senior staff and her responsibilities include planning and organizing large-
19 scale events, such as the Military Ball; and

1 WHEREAS, Miranda also has a passion for dance and has had the privilege of student
2 teaching at the Pointe Dance Studio, working with younger students. Miranda is also actively
3 involved at Hopkins Hill Elementary School, where she volunteers her time to assist with the
4 Community Learning Academy, providing support and guidance to students in need of extra
5 assistance. Miranda has also contributed to an anti-bullying program within the physical
6 education department; and
7 WHEREAS, Miranda’s other activities include being an active member of the St. Francis
8 Altar Guild, the Ladies Auxiliary of the Disabled American Veterans, and the Westwood YMCA;
9 now, therefore be it
10 RESOLVED, That this Senate of the State of Rhode Island hereby honors and recognizes
11 Miranda Marie Musumeci for being named Miss Ireland in the West Warwick St. Patrick’s Day
12 Parade 2026 and extends sincere appreciation for her dedication to Irish heritage and community
13 pride; and be it further
14 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
15 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Miranda Marie Musumeci.
