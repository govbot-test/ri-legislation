S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D

JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO HOLIDAYS AND DAYS OF SPECIAL OBSERVANCE
Introduced By: Representatives McGaw, Boylan, Handy, Fogarty, Carson, Kislak,
Alzate, Cotter, and Speakman
Date Introduced: January 21, 2026
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 25-2 of the General Laws entitled "Days of Special Observance" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 25-2-62. Rhode Island native plant month.
4 The month of May shall annually be set apart as a month to be known as "Rhode Island
5 Native Plant Month." The month shall be observed by the people of this state with appropriate
6 ceremonies and activities to recognize as well as educate people about the importance and history
7 of Rhode Island's native plants and the need to preserve them.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
