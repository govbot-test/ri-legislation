S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D

JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO CRIMINAL OFFENSES -- SEXUAL ASSAULT
Introduced By: Representatives Roberts, Hull, McNamara, Edwards, Bennett, Casey,
Solomon, Serpa, Azzinaro, and Costantino
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Sections 11-37-3 and 11-37-8.2 of the General Laws in Chapter 11-37 entitled
2 "Sexual Assault" are hereby amended to read as follows:
3 11-37-3. Penalty for first degree sexual assault.
4 Every person who shall commit sexual assault in the first degree shall be imprisoned for a
5 period not less than ten (10) years and may be imprisoned for life; provided, however, that the
6 sentencing court shall ensure that the victim or victims approve of any negotiated sentence imposed
7 on the offender.
8 11-37-8.2. Penalty for first degree child molestation sexual assault.
9 Every person who shall commit first degree child molestation sexual assault shall be
10 imprisoned for a period of not less than twenty-five (25) years and may be imprisoned for life;
11 provided, however, that the sentencing court shall ensure that the victim or victims, or their parents
12 or guardian, approve of the any negotiated sentence imposed on the offender.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO CRIMINAL OFFENSES -- SEXUAL ASSAULT
***
1 This act would require that any court sentencing a person for first degree sexual assault or
2 first degree child molestation, have the approval of the victim before imposing any negotiated
3 sentence upon the offender.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
