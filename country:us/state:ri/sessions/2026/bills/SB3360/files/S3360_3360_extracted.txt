____________
A N A C T
AUTHORIZING THE AMENDMENT OF THE WARWICK CITY CHARTER TO ALLOW
FOR ENACTMENT OF ORDINANCES THAT SUBJECT MAYORAL APPOINTMENTS TO
CERTAIN CODIFIED CITY BOARDS AND COMMISSIONS TO THE ADVICE AND
CONSENT OF THE CITY COUNCIL
Introduced By: Senators McKenney, Appollonio, and LaMountain
Date Introduced: June 05, 2026
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Pursuant to the request of the Warwick City Council by resolution adopted
2 on May 11, 2026, Article VIII of the Warwick City Charter is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 8-23. – Council Advice and Consent to Boards and Commissions.
4 Notwithstanding any provisions of this charter to the contrary, and except for the Board of
5 Public Safety, the city council may enact ordinances that subject mayoral appointments to city
6 boards and commissions specifically codified in the charter or the code of ordinances to the advice
7 and consent of the city council.
8 SECTION 2. Pursuant to Article 10, Section 29 of the Warwick City Charter, these
9 amendments shall be submitted to the voters of the city at the next general election to be held in
10 November, 2026, or a special election as may be scheduled sooner, for approval or rejection thereof,
11 in the following form:
12 1. Shall the city council be authorized to enact ordinances that subject mayoral
13 appointments to city boards and commissions specifically codified in the charter or the code of
14 ordinances, except for the Board of Public Safety, to the advice and consent of the city council?
15 APPROVE DISAPPROVE
16 SECTION 3. Section 2 of this act shall take effect upon passage. Section 1 of this act shall

1 take effect upon approval by the voters of the city of Warwick at a general or special election.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
AUTHORIZING THE AMENDMENT OF THE WARWICK CITY CHARTER TO ALLOW
FOR ENACTMENT OF ORDINANCES THAT SUBJECT MAYORAL APPOINTMENTS TO
CERTAIN CODIFIED CITY BOARDS AND COMMISSIONS TO THE ADVICE AND
CONSENT OF THE CITY COUNCIL
***
1 This act would approve charter amendments to the Warwick city charter subject to voter
2 approval at the general election to be held in November, 2026, or at a special election as may be
3 scheduled sooner. If approved, these amendments would authorize the city council to enact
4 ordinances that subject mayoral appointments to city boards and commissions specifically codified
5 in the charter or the code of ordinances, to the advice and consent of the city council.
6 Section 2 of this act would take effect upon passage. Section 1 of this act would take effect
7 upon approval by the voters of the city of Warwick at a general or special election.
