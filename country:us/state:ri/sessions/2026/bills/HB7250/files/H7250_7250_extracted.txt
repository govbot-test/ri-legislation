____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC FINANCE -- STATE BUDGET
Introduced By: Representatives Nardone, Quattrocchi, Fascia, Santucci, Paplauskas,
Chippendale, Place, and Hopkins
Date Introduced: January 21, 2026
Referred To: House Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 35-3-2 of the General Laws in Chapter 35-3 entitled "State Budget"
2 is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 35-3-2. Annual appropriations for state government.
4 The general assembly shall annually appropriate such sums as it may deem necessary to
5 pay the administrative and other expenses of the state government. The general assembly shall
6 adopt a spending cap on general budget expenditures equal to the greater of:
7 (1) The average five (5) year increase in personal income; or
8 (2) The annual increase in inflation per the consumer price index.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
