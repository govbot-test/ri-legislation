A N A C T
RELATING TO GENERAL ASSEMBLY -- JOINT COMMITTEE ON LEGISLATIVE
SERVICES
Introduced By: Senators Tikoian, de la Cruz, Lawson, Ciccone, Bissaillon, Britto, Burke,
Sosnowski, LaMountain, and Felag
Date Introduced: March 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Special Legislation and Veterans Affairs
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 22-11-1 of the General Laws in Chapter 22-11 entitled "Joint
2 Committee on Legislative Services" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 22-11-1. Permanent committee — Composition.
4 There is created a permanent joint committee on legislative services consisting of the
5 president, majority, and the minority leader leaders of the senate, the majority and minority leaders
6 from the house of representatives, and the speaker of the house of representatives. The members of
7 the committee shall serve until their successors have been duly elected and qualified.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
