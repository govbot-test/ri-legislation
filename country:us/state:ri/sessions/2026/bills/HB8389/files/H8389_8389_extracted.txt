JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- UNIVERSITY OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Fogarty, Carson, Tanzi, Caldwell, McEntee, Lima, Hull,
McGaw, Spears, and Dawson
Date Introduced: April 01, 2026
Referred To: House Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The general assembly hereby finds and declares that:
2 (1) Rhode Island faces an ongoing and worsening projected shortage of physicians and
3 healthcare professionals, particularly in primary care and underserved communities;
4 (2) Establishing a medical school at the University of Rhode Island would strengthen the
5 state's healthcare workforce pipeline, improve access to care, and support long-term economic
6 development; and
7 (3) An initial state investment is necessary to support leadership recruitment, faculty hiring,
8 accreditation planning, and curriculum development for such medical school.
9 SECTION 2. Chapter 16-32 of the General Laws entitled "University of Rhode Island [See
10 Title 16 Chapter 97 — The Rhode Island Board of Education Act]" is hereby amended by adding
11 thereto the following section:
12 16-32-43. University of Rhode Island medical school -- Establishment and funding.
13 (a) For fiscal year 2027, the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) is hereby appropriated
14 from general revenues to the University of Rhode Island for the purpose of initiating the
15 establishment of a medical school.
16 (b) Funds appropriated pursuant to subsection (a) of this section shall be used for operating
17 expenses and salaries related to, including, but not limited to:
18 (1) Hiring a founding dean and senior leadership;
19 (2) Recruitment of core faculty and administrative staff;

1 (3) Accreditation preparation and compliance activities; and
2 (4) Curriculum planning and institutional development.
3 (c) It is the intent of the general assembly that the fiscal year 2027 appropriation represents
4 the first phase of a multi-year state investment to support the development of a medical school at
5 the University of Rhode Island.
6 (d) Subject to future appropriations, the general assembly anticipates additional funding in
7 the following amounts:
8 (1) Fiscal year 2028: Seven million dollars ($7,000,000); and
9 (2) Fiscal year 2029: Eight million dollars ($8,000,000).
10 (e) Nothing in this section shall be construed as requiring any future general assembly to
11 appropriate such funds.
12 (f) The University of Rhode Island shall submit a report to the governor and the general
13 assembly no later than January 1, 2028, detailing:
14 (1) The use of funds appropriated under this section;
15 (2) Progress toward accreditation and institutional readiness;
16 (3) Faculty and leadership hiring progress; and
17 (4) A projected timeline and budget for subsequent phases of development.
18 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- UNIVERSITY OF RHODE ISLAND
***
1 This act would establish the framework to create a medical school at the University of
2 Rhode Island and provide an initial appropriation of five million dollars ($5,000,000) as the first
3 phase of a multi-year investment for the development of a medical school.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
