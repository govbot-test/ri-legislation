EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- ACCIDENTS AND ACCIDENT
REPORTS -- THE MATTHEW O'GARA ACT OF 2026
***
1 This act would increase the penalties for a first offense of leaving the scene of an accident
2 resulting in death to a minimum ten (10) years in incarceration, a minimum of ten thousand dollars
3 ($10,000) and a minimum license revocation of ten (10) years. It would also increase other penalties
4 in this section from a minimum of five (5) years to ten (10) years in prison for violation.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
