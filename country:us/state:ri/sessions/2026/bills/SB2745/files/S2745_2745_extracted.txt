____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO GENERAL ASSEMBLY -- PERMANENT JOINT COMMITTEE ON
NAMING ALL NEW BUILDINGS, BRIDGES, EDIFICES AND OTHER STATE
CONSTRUCTIONS
Introduced By: Senators de la Cruz, DiMario, Lauria, and Rogers
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: Senate Special Legislation and Veterans Affairs
(by request)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 22-7.4 of the General Laws entitled "Permanent Joint Committee on
2 Naming All New Buildings, Bridges, Edifices and Other State Constructions" is hereby amended
3 by adding thereto the following section:
4 22-7.4-160. Joseph M. Lima Overpass.
5 The India Point Park Overpass pedestrian bridge spanning the highway in the City of
6 Providence shall hereafter be named and known as the "Joseph M. Lima Overpass" in recognition
7 of his lifetime of selfless public service and his enduring impact on the Fox Point neighborhood
8 and the State of Rhode Island.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
