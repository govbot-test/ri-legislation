A N A C T
RELATING TO FOOD AND DRUGS -- THE RHODE ISLAND KRATOM ACT
Introduced By: Senators Lauria, Euer, DiMario, Ujifusa, Felag, Zurier, Valverde,
Kallman, and McKenney
Date Introduced: February 06, 2026
Referred To: Senate Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 21-28.12-4 of the General Laws in Chapter 21-28.12 entitled "The
2 Rhode Island Kratom Act [Effective April 1, 2026.]" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 21-28.12-4. Age limits and sales restrictions. [Effective April 1, 2026.]
4 (a) No person or entity shall distribute, sell, transfer, or advertise a kratom product to a
5 person under twenty-one (21) years.
6 (b) All kratom products shall be kept behind the sales counter and within a locked case,
7 which case would only accessible to store employees until the kratom product is sold.
8 (c) The sale or distribution of kratom or kratom products to individuals under the age of
9 twenty-one (21) is prohibited.
10 (d) Valid photo identification for proof of age is required for all kratom and kratom product
11 purchases. Acceptable forms of government-issued identification shall include a recent photo of
12 the individual to whom the identification has been issued showing the date of birth of the individual
13 to verify the individual is age twenty-one (21) years or over. Acceptable forms of government-
14 issued identification are: a valid motor vehicle operator’s license issued by the state of Rhode Island
15 or another state; a valid driver privilege card issued by the state of Rhode Island or another state; a
16 valid Rhode Island identification card issued by the administrator of the division of motor vehicles;
17 a valid armed services identification card; or a valid passport.

1 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on July 1, 2027.
