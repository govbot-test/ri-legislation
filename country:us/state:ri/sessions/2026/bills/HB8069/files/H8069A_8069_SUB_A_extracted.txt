A N A C T
RELATING TO CRIMINAL OFFENSES -- WEAPONS
Introduced By: Representatives Donovan, Boylan, Tanzi, Alzate, Speakman, Cortvriend,
Casimiro, Cruz, Furtado, and Potter
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 11-47 of the General Laws entitled "Weapons" is hereby amended
2 by adding thereto the following section:
3 11-47-60.5. Suicide awareness at shooting ranges.
4 (a) For purposes of this section:
5 (1) “Firearm” shall have the same definition as provided in § 11-55-1; and
6 (2) “Shooting range” means a rifle, pistol, silhouette, skeet, trap, black powder, or other
7 similar range designed and operated for the use of firearms on site, including any range located on
8 public or private land or operated by a public or private entity.
9 (b) Every shooting range shall conspicuously display a sign containing the information set
10 forth is subsection (c) of this section, in any areas where shooting activity occurs. The required
11 signs shall be posted in a manner in order that they can be easily viewed by people utilizing the
12 shooting range and shall not be removed, obscured or rendered illegible.
13 (c) Each sign shall be at least eight and one-half inches (8.5”) high by eleven inches (11”)
14 wide and feature black text against a white background and letters that are in at least thirty-six (36)
15 point font. The signs shall contain the following text, in English and Spanish: “WARNING: If you
16 or a loved one is experiencing distress and/or depression, call the 988 Suicide and Crisis hotline or
17 text “HOME” to 741741”. The signs shall not contain any other text.
18 (d) A shooting range that operates on a membership basis shall require any person who is
19 not a member of the shooting range, to be accompanied at all times by a member of the range or

1 staff member of the range while on the premises and while engaging in shooting activity.
2 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO CRIMINAL OFFENSES -- WEAPONS
***
1 This act would require any shooting range operating in this state to post signs in any area
2 in which shooting occurs that contain the following language: “WARNING: If you or a loved one
3 is experiencing distress and/or depression, call the 988 Suicide and Crisis hotline or text “HOME”
4 to 741741” and the signs would not contain any other text.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
