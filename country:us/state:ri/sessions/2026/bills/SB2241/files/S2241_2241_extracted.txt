A N A C T
RELATING TO HUMAN SERVICES -- MEDICAL ASSISTANCE
Introduced By: Senators DiMario, Lauria, Zurier, Acosta, Murray, Thompson, Quezada,
Kallman, Britto, and Mack
Date Introduced: January 23, 2026
Referred To: Senate Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 40-8 of the General Laws entitled "Medical Assistance" is hereby
2 amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 40-8-33. Graduate student services covered.
4 Notwithstanding any general law, public law, special law, rule or regulation to the contrary,
5 Medicaid reimbursement shall be provided under this chapter for services provided by a graduate
6 student intern in one of the mental health professional disciplines who is formally assigned by an
7 accredited college/university to an agency or facility for clinical training. The graduate student
8 intern shall be allowed to receive compensation from these reimbursed hours and the supervising
9 organization is allowed to retain a portion of the compensation to cover expenses related to
10 supervisory work. A mental health practitioner working as a clinical trainee shall:
11 (1) Comply with requirements for licensure or board certification as a mental health
12 professional including supervised practice in the delivery of mental health services for the treatment
13 of mental illness; or
14 (2) Be a graduate student in a bona fide field placement or internship under a program
15 leading to completion of the requirements for licensure as a mental health professional.

1 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
