A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES AND CARRIERS -- DUTIES OF UTILITIES AND
CARRIERS
Introduced By: Representatives Hull, J. Lombardi, Fogarty, DeSimone, Tanzi, Felix,
Azzinaro, Alzate, Bennett, and Slater
Date Introduced: January 28, 2026
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 39-2 of the General Laws entitled "Duties of Utilities and Carriers"
2 is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 39-2-29. Duty to maintain customer service operations within the state.
4 Every public utility operated and licensed to provide services to the residents and
5 businesses within the state under chapter 1 of title 39, shall maintain a customer service operation
6 physically located within the state which is reasonably staffed to meet the expectations of the
7 public. The customer service operation shall be available to answer customer inquiries, meet with
8 customers, accept payment of bills, and perform any other services that the public utilities
9 commission may direct from time to time.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
