S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
RESPECTFULLY REQUESTING THAT A RECYCLING/WASTE AUDIT BE CONDUCTED
ON THE RHODE ISLAND STATE HOUSE WHICH PROVIDES RECOMMENDATIONS
FOR A WASTE REDUCTION STRATEGY
Introduced By: Representatives Carson, McGaw, Fogarty, Boylan, Cortvriend,
Speakman, Spears, and Cotter
Date Introduced: February 06, 2026
Referred To: House Environment and Natural Resources
1 WHEREAS, A waste audit is a method/survey for analyzing a facility's waste stream.
2 The goal is to identify what is being thrown away, what is being recycled or diverted through
3 other means, what can be composted and/or recovered, and the amounts of each type (paper,
4 plastic, food etc.) by weight or volume that are produced within a given timeframe; and
5 WHEREAS, Waste audits can reveal costly, wasteful problems and verifies what is being
6 thrown away, and the associated costs monetarily and environmentally; now, therefore be it
7 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
8 conducts a recycling/waste audit on the Rhode Island State House, provides recommendations for
9 a waste reduction strategy, and reports back to the House on or before January 5, 2027; and be it
10 further
11 RESOLVED, That this House hereby further requests that the Executive Director of the
12 Joint Committee on Legislative Services and the Division of Capital Asset Management and
13 Maintenance (DCAMM) investigate the installation of water filling stations and the installation of
14 electric car chargers in the State House parking lots, in order to reduce the use of plastic water
15 bottles and to meet the carbon emission goals of the Act on Climate, and who would report back
16 to the House on or before January 5, 2027; and be it further
17 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
18 transmit duly certified copies of this resolution to the Governor of the State of Rhode Island, the
19 Joint Committee on Legislative Services, and the Division of Capital Asset Management and

1 Maintenance.
