A N A C T
RELATING TO LABOR AND LABOR RELATIONS -- FAIR EMPLOYMENT PRACTICES
Introduced By: Senators Murray, Thompson, DiPalma, Ciccone, Acosta, and McKenney
Date Introduced: March 04, 2026
Referred To: Senate Labor & Gaming
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 28-5 of the General Laws entitled "Fair Employment Practices" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 28-5-44. Labor and payment of debts by contractors.
4 Any contract for capital improvements to any healthcare facility as defined in § 23-17-2
5 including, but not limited to, construction, reconstruction, renovation, demolition, and alteration,
6 shall adhere to the requirements and obligations in chapter 13 of title 37 which requires all relevant
7 contractors and subcontractors performing work on the healthcare facility to pay their employees,
8 as defined in § 37-13-7.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
