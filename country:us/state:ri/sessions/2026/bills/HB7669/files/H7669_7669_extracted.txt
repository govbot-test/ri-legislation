A N A C T
RELATING TO ELECTIONS -- CONDUCT OF ELECTION AND VOTING EQUIPMENT,
AND SUPPLIES
Introduced By: Representatives J. Brien, Corvese, Chippendale, Noret, and Hopkins
Date Introduced: February 11, 2026
Referred To: House State Government & Elections
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 17-19 of the General Laws entitled "Conduct of Election and Voting
2 Equipment, and Supplies" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 17-19-24.4. Early voting.
4 Early voting shall be limited to a total of ten (10) nonconsecutive days preceding election
5 day and shall include the weekend days immediately preceding the election and eight (8) weekdays.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
