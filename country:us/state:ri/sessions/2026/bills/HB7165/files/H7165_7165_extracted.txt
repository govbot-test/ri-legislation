____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
Introduced By: Representatives Chippendale, Blazejewski, and Shekarchi
Date Introduced: January 20, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any other general or special law to the contrary, the
2 Honorable Mayor Frank Picozzi may join Madeline Marie Hopkins and Cameron David Charron
3 in marriage within the City of Warwick, Rhode Island on or about February 10, 2026. The
4 Honorable Mayor Frank Picozzi is hereby authorized and empowered to join the foregoing persons
5 in marriage pursuant to and in accordance with Chapter 3 of Title 15 of the General laws, entitled
6 “Solemnization of Marriages.”
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
