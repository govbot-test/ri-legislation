A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- PASSING, USE OF LANES, AND
RULES OF THE ROAD
Introduced By: Senators Paolino, Lauria, de la Cruz, Acosta, and Bissaillon
Date Introduced: February 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 31-15-2 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-15 entitled "Passing, Use
2 of Lanes, and Rules of the Road" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 31-15-2. Slow traffic to right.
4 (a) Upon all roadways any vehicle proceeding at less than the normal speed of traffic at the
5 time and place and under the conditions then existing shall be driven in the right-hand lane then
6 available for traffic, or as close as practicable to the right-hand curb or edge of the roadway, except
7 when overtaking and passing another vehicle proceeding in the same direction or when preparing
8 for a left turn at an intersection or into a private road or driveway. Violations of this section are
9 subject to fines enumerated in § 31-41.1-4.
10 (b) On any multi-lane, limited access highway, the extreme left-hand lane shall be reserved
11 for passing slower vehicles only, and occupation of that lane when not passing shall be a violation
12 of this section and subject to the fines enumerated in § 31-41.1-4.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
