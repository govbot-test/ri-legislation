A N A C T
RELATING TO COURTS AND CIVIL PROCEDURE -- PROCEDURE GENERALLY --
JUDGMENTS, ORDERS, AND DECREES
Introduced By: Senators Famiglietti, Murray, Thompson, Dimitri, Appollonio, Patalano,
Bissaillon, Bell, Paolino, and Burke
Date Introduced: January 16, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 9-21 of the General Laws entitled "Judgments, Orders, and Decrees"
2 is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 9-21-14. Judgment lien notice.
4 (a) Notwithstanding any such notice provided through the court process, within ten (10)
5 business days of recording a judgment lien on real property, the judgment creditor shall send written
6 notice to the property owner by certified mail at the last known address.
7 (b) The notice shall include:
8 (1) The name and address of the judgment creditor;
9 (2) The amount of the judgment;
10 (3) The property description affected by the lien; and
11 (4) Instructions on how to satisfy or contest the lien.
12 (c) Failure to provide the required notice shall not invalidate the lien but may toll any
13 deadlines related to enforcement until notice is given.
14 (d) The clerk of the court shall maintain records of judgment lien filings and notices sent.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on January 1, 2027.
