S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO CRIMINAL OFFENSES -- WEAPONS
Introduced By: Representatives Hopkins, Casimiro, Casey, Noret, J. Brien, and Place
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 11-47-42 of the General Laws in Chapter 11-47 entitled "Weapons"
2 is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 11-47-42. Weapons other than firearms prohibited.
4 (a)(1) No person shall carry or possess or attempt to use against another any instrument or
5 weapon of the kind commonly known as a blackjack, slingshot, billy, sandclub, sandbag, metal
6 knuckles, slap glove, bludgeon, stun-gun, or the so called “Kung-Fu” weapons; provided, however,
7 that any student, professor, or other employee of any public or private college or university, may
8 carry, possess and have in their custody, possession and/or under their control, a stun-gun or pepper
9 spray for purposes of self-defense.
10 (2) No person shall with intent to use unlawfully against another, carry or possess a
11 crossbow, dagger, dirk, stiletto, sword-in-cane, bowie knife, or other similar weapon designed to
12 cut and stab another.
13 (3) No person shall wear or carry concealed upon his person, any of the above-mentioned
14 instruments or weapons, or any razor, or knife of any description having a blade of more than three
15 (3) inches in length measuring from the end of the handle where the blade is attached to the end of
16 the blade, or other weapon of like kind or description.
17 Any person violating the provisions of these subsections shall be punished by a fine of not
18 more than one thousand dollars ($1,000) or by imprisonment for not more than one year, or both,
19 and the weapon so found shall be confiscated.

1 Any person violating the provisions of these subsections while he or she is incarcerated
2 within the confines of the adult correctional institutions shall be punished by a fine of not less than
3 one thousand dollars ($1,000) nor more than three thousand dollars ($3,000), or by imprisonment
4 for not less than one year nor more than five (5) years, or both, and the weapon so found shall be
5 confiscated.
6 (b) No person shall sell to a person under eighteen (18) years of age, without the written
7 authorization of the minor’s parent or legal guardian, any stink bomb, blackjack, slingshot, bill,
8 sandclub, sandbag, metal knuckles, slap glove, bludgeon, stungun, paint ball gun, so called “kung-
9 fu” weapons, dagger, dirk, stiletto, sword-in-cane, bowie knife, razor, or knife of any description
10 having a blade of more than three inches (3″) in length as described in subsection (a) of this section,
11 or any multi-pronged star with sharpened edges designed to be used as a weapon and commonly
12 known as a Chinese throwing star, except that an individual who is actually engaged in the
13 instruction of martial arts and licensed under § 5-43-1 [repealed] may carry and possess any multi-
14 pronged star with sharpened edges for the sole purpose of instructional use. Any person violating
15 the provisions of this subsection shall be punished by a fine of not less than one thousand dollars
16 ($1,000) nor more than three thousand dollars ($3,000), or by imprisonment for not less than one
17 year nor more than five (5) years, or both, and the weapons so found shall be confiscated.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

2 public or private college or university, to carry, possess and have in their custody, possession and/or
3 under their control, a stun-gun or pepper spray for purposes of self-defense.
