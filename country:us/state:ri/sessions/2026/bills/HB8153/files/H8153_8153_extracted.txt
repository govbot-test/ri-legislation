A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- PAYMENTS TO FIRE COMPANIES
Introduced By: Representatives Cotter, and Casimiro
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-18 of the General Laws entitled "Payments to Fire Companies"
2 is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-18-4. Payments to Exeter fire and rescue service.
4 The Exeter Volunteer Fire Department No. 1 and Exeter Volunteer Fire Company 2, as
5 well as services provided by the Exeter ambulance transport, EMT intermediate, and paramedic
6 care, shall be reimbursed by the state for seventy-five percent (75%) of the costs for any calls made
7 on state property.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
