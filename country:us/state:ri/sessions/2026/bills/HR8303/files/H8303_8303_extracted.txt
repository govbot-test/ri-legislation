S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
PROCLAIMING MARCH OF 2026, TO BE "BRAIN INJURY AWARENESS MONTH" IN
THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Furtado, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Kazarian,
Chippendale, Read, Tanzi, Fogarty, Ackerman, and Alzate
Date Introduced: March 17, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, The Brain Injury Association of Rhode Island has identified that more than
2 12,000 residents of Rhode Island will sustain a traumatic brain injury this year; and
3 WHEREAS, The Brain Injury Association of America has identified brain injury as the
4 leading cause of death and disability among children and young adults in this country; and
5 WHEREAS, Brain injury is an extremely costly public health issue in the State of Rhode
6 Island; and
7 WHEREAS, Advances in medical technology have enabled many persons with severe
8 brain injury to survive these injuries; and
9 WHEREAS, Persons with brain injury may need specialized rehabilitation programs and
10 other services to meet specific needs to live life to maximum potential; and
11 WHEREAS, Most forms of insurance, public and private, that are available to persons
12 with brain injuries may not cover the rehabilitative and long-term care needs of these individuals;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, People with brain injury and their families too often must choose financial
15 hardship and discontinuing critical treatment and services due to the substantial long-term costs;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, Without specialized community services, people with brain injury may
18 become inappropriately institutionalized; and
19 WHEREAS, Providing treatment methods and services to people with brain injury must

1 be a priority for the State of Rhode Island; and
2 WHEREAS, Thousands of Rhode Islanders are affected by diseases of the brain, and
3 brain injuries both acquired and traumatic including concussion, brain cancer, epilepsy, and other
4 neurological disorders; now, therefore be it
5 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
6 proclaims March of 2026 to be "Brain Injury Awareness Month" in Rhode Island. We
7 furthermore encourage all Rhode Islanders to participate in the month-long planned activities
8 highlighting the wonders of the brain, and the observation of Brain Injury Awareness Month; and
9 be it further
10 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
11 transmit a duly certified copy of this Resolution to Neil Sharpe, Executive Director of the Brain
12 Injury Association of Rhode Island.
