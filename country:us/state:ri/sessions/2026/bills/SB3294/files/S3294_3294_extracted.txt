____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
Introduced By: Senators Felag, Lauria, Ujifusa, and DiPalma
Date Introduced: May 15, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any other general or special law to the contrary, Joseph
2 Couto may join Brandon Kemmy and Jacqueline Manousos in marriage within the Town of
3 Tiverton, Rhode Island on or about August 2, 2026. Joseph Couto is hereby authorized and
4 empowered to join the foregoing persons in marriage pursuant to and in accordance with Chapter
5 3 of Title 15 of the General laws, entitled "Solemnization of Marriages."
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
