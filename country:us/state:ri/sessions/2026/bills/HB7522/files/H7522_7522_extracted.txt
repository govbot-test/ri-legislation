Referred To: House Corporations
(by request)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Findings.
2 The General Assembly hereby finds and declares that:
3 (1) There continues to exist uncertainty as to the ownership of streetlights located within
4 the Quonset Business Park;
5 (2) Uncertainty as to the ownership of streetlights located within the Quonset Business
6 Park has hindered the efficient and effective use, maintenance, and care of said streetlights and thus
7 compromised the safe passage of motorists and pedestrians traversing therein;
8 (3) In order to streamline the use, maintenance, and care of streetlights located within the
9 Quonset Business Park and to ensure motorist and pedestrian safety therein, the General Assembly
10 hereby enacts the following legislation to permit the Quonset Development Corporation to acquire,
11 own, maintain, and control all streetlights within the Quonset Business Park.
12 SECTION 2. Chapter 39-30 of the General Laws entitled "Municipal Streetlight
13 Investment Act" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
14 39-30-6. Lighting equipment located within the Quonset Business Park.
15 Notwithstanding the provisions of this chapter, the Quonset Development Corporation
16 shall be permitted to acquire, own, maintain, and control all of the lighting equipment, including
17 streetlights, located within the Quonset Business Park. If any lighting equipment located within
18 the Quonset Business Park is owned at the time of passage of this act by the electric distribution

1 company or the municipality where the lighting equipment is located, the Quonset Development
2 Corporation shall be afforded the same rights and abilities as the municipalities of the state are
3 afforded under § 39-30-3 and shall be permitted to acquire such lighting equipment from the electric
4 distribution company or the municipality, whichever the case may be; provided that, the Quonset
5 Development Corporation compensates the electric distribution company or the municipality as
6 necessary in accordance with the requirements set forth in § 39-30-3(b).
