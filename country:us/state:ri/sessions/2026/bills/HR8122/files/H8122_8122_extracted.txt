S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
AMENDING THE RULES OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES FOR THE YEARS
2025 AND 2026
Introduced By: Representatives Serpa, Fellela, Cotter, Messier, Corvese, and Knight
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Rules
1 RESOLVED, That section (38) of the rules of the House of Representatives adopted by
2 the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2025, entitled “House Resolution
3 Adopting the Rules of the House of Representatives for the years 2025 and 2026”, be and the
4 same is hereby amended to read as follows:
5 Rules Pertaining to Admission to the Floor
6 (38)(a). No person or persons, except currently elected members of the general assembly,
7 legislative staff assigned by the Speaker or Minority Leader and authorized representatives of the
8 public press shall be admitted to the floor of the House during the session thereof, except by the
9 approval of the Speaker for a designated purpose. All guests and lobbyists shall vacate the house
10 floor twenty (20) minutes prior to the beginning of the scheduled opening of the House session,
11 unless by approval of the speaker. The Speaker may make special provision for admission to the
12 floor of the House during the session thereof for people who, by reason of disability, are unable
13 to gain access to the House galleries. All persons so admitted by the Speaker to the floor of the
14 House during the session thereof shall be present for the sole purpose of observing the
15 proceedings of the House and shall remain seated, refrain from conversation, and maintain the
16 decorum of the House. No person so admitted shall contact, address, speak or gesture to, or
17 communicate in any way with any House member while present on the floor of the House. No
18 person on the floor of the chamber shall dress in a manner offensive to the decorum of the House.
19 Any House member who observes conduct in violation of the House Rules shall immediately

1 notify the Speaker thereof and the Speaker shall forthwith take appropriate corrective action and
2 may order the removal of the offending person.
3 (b) During House sessions, admission to the House lounge is limited to currently serving
4 members, and staff of the General Assembly authorized by the Speaker
5 (c) Complimentary items, souvenirs and gifts of food shall not be placed upon members'
6 desks nor delivered to the floor of the House or to members' mailboxes.
