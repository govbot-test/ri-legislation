S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
COMMENDING THE SOCIETY FOR ADVANCEMENT OF MANAGEMENT (SAM) ON
THE 2026 81ST ANNUAL INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS CONFERENCE, MARCH 16–21,
2026, AND RECOGNIZING CONFERENCE CHAIR, SHELBY PERRY
Introduced By: Representatives Caldwell, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Chippendale,
Kennedy, Tanzi, Ajello, Fogarty, Solomon, and Shallcross Smith
Date Introduced: March 12, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, The Society for Advancement of Management (SAM), founded in 1912, has
2 for more than a century advanced the principles and practice of management through education,
3 scholarship, and professional development; and
4 WHEREAS, The Society for Advancement of Management will bring the 2026 81st
5 Annual International Business Conference to the State of Rhode Island, hosted by the New
6 England Institute of Technology in East Greenwich from March 16 through March 21, 2026; and
7 WHEREAS, This conference will provide scholars, practitioners, and students an
8 opportunity to share research, innovation, and best practices in management and organizational
9 leadership; and
10 WHEREAS, The presence of this international conference is expected to enhance Rhode
11 Island’s reputation for academic excellence, professional development, and organizational
12 leadership; and
13 WHEREAS, Shelby Perry, serving as one of the 2026 Conference Chair, has
14 demonstrated exceptional leadership, vision, and dedication in planning, organizing, and
15 coordinating this important event; and
16 WHEREAS, Through her tireless efforts, Shelby Perry has ensured that the conference
17 will facilitate meaningful collaboration among scholars, practitioners, and students, highlight
18 Rhode Island as a center for academic and professional excellence, and set a high standard for
19 future conferences; and

1 WHEREAS, Shelby Perry’s professionalism, creativity, and commitment to the
2 management profession exemplify outstanding service and bring distinction to the State of Rhode
3 Island; now, therefore be it
4 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
5 commends the Society for Advancement of Management and the New England Institute of
6 Technology for bringing the 2026 81st Annual International Business Conference to Rhode
7 Island, and extends best wishes for a successful and impactful conference; and be it further
8 RESOLVED, That this House furthermore recognizes and commends Shelby Perry for
9 her exemplary leadership, vision, and dedicated service in planning, coordinating, and ensuring
10 the success of the 2026 Society for Advancement of Management International Business
11 Conference; and be it further
12 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
13 transmit duly certified copies of this resolution to the Society for Advancement of Management
14 (SAM), the New England Institute of Technology, and Shelby Perry.
