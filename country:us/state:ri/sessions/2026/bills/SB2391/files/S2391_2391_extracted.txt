A N A C T
RELATING TO PROPERTY -- PUBLIC AUCTIONS OF REAL ESTATE
Introduced By: Senators Patalano, Dimitri, Ciccone, LaMountain, Tikoian, Famiglietti,
Quezada, Appollonio, Thompson, and Raptakis
Date Introduced: January 30, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Purpose
2 The purpose of this act is to bring greater public scrutiny to the public auction sales of real
3 estate in Rhode Island.
4 SECTION 2. Title 34 of the General Laws entitled "PROPERTY" is hereby amended by
5 adding thereto the following chapter:
6 CHAPTER 51
7 PUBLIC AUCTIONS OF REAL ESTATE
8 34-51-1. Public auctions of real estate.
9 (a) Except as provided in subsection (b) of this section, public auctions by any city or town
10 of real estate or an interest therein shall only be done in person at the time and place specified in
11 the legal advertisement, and in accordance with Rhode Island statutes that govern said sales, to
12 include, but not be limited to, the provisions of chapter 9 of title 44.
13 (b) No public auction of real estate or an interest therein shall be done by electronic or
14 remote procedures unless authorized by court order of the Rhode Island superior court, after hearing
15 thereon and a finding that an in-person auction is not feasible under the circumstances of the
16 specific case.

1 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.
