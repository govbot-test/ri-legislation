S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D

JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING THE REPORTING AND EXPIRATION DATES OF THE LEGISLATIVE
STUDY COMMISSION ON CLIMATE CHANGE IMPACTS AND SOLUTIONS
Introduced By: Representatives Cortvriend, Spears, and Boylan
Date Introduced: January 28, 2026
Referred To: House Environment and Natural Resources
1 RESOLVED, That the special House commission created by resolution No. 361 passed
2 by the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2024, and approved June 11, 2024,
3 entitled "House Resolution Creating a Legislative Study Commission On Climate Change
4 Impacts and Solutions," as further amended by resolution No. 161 passed by the House of
5 Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2025, and approved March 18, 2025, entitled "House
6 Resolution Extending the Reporting and Expiration Dates and Amending the Membership of the
7 Legislative Study Commission on Climate Change Impact and Solutions," is hereby authorized to
8 continue its study and make a report to the House of Representatives on or before May 14, 2029,
9 and may issue interim reports as the commission deems fit or recommends as appropriate prior to
10 that date, and said commission shall expire on June 18, 2029; and be it further
11 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting authorized by resolution No. 361 passed by the
12 House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2024, and approved June 11, 2024, and as
13 extended by resolution No. 161 passed by the House of Representatives at its January session,
14 A.D. 2025, and approved March 18, 2025, be and the same is hereby rescinded.
