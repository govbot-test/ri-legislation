A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
Introduced By: Senator V. Susan Sosnowski
Date Introduced: May 22, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any other general or special law to the contrary, Kimberley
2 Gaffett may join Bonnie Phyllis Nolan and David Gaffett Spier in marriage within the Town of
3 New Shoreham, on or about October 3, 2026. Kimberley Gaffett is hereby authorized and
4 empowered to join the foregoing persons in marriage pursuant to and in accordance with Chapter
5 3 of Title 15 of the General Laws, entitled “Solemnization of Marriages.”
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
