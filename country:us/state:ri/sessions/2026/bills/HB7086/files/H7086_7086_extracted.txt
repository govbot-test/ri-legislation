A N A C T
RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS AND GOVERNMENT -- STATE EMBLEMS
Introduced By: Representatives Bennett, Fogarty, Cortvriend, Donovan, Fellela,
McNamara, Azzinaro, Speakman, Boylan, and Read
Date Introduced: January 14, 2026
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 42-4 of the General Laws entitled "State Emblems" is hereby
2 amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 42-4-24. State music.
4 The music genre of rhythm and blues is hereby designated as the state music. For purposes
5 of this section, rhythm and blues is defined as music which contains elements of pop, soul, funk,
6 hip-hop and electronic music.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
