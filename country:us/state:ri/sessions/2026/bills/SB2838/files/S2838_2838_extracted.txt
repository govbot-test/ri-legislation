A N A C T
RELATING TO HOLIDAYS AND DAYS OF SPECIAL OBSERVANCE-- DAYS OF
SPECIAL OBSERVANCE
Introduced By: Senator David P. Tikoian
Date Introduced: March 04, 2026
Referred To: Senate Special Legislation and Veterans Affairs
(Adm/Office of Healthy Aging (OHA))
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 25-2 of the General Laws entitled "Days of Special Observance" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 25-2-62. Rhode Island senior center month.
4 The month of September in each and every year shall be set aside as a month to be known
5 as "Rhode Island Senior Center Month." Rhode Island senior centers act as catalysts for mobilizing
6 the creativity, energy, vitality, and commitment of older residents to the State of Rhode Island. This
7 month shall be observed by the people of this state with appropriate exercises in public places.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
