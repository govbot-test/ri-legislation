S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION OF $5,000,000 TO THE RHODE ISLAND PUBLIC
TRANSIT AUTHORITY
Introduced By: Senators McKenney, Appollonio, Urso, Bissaillon, Zurier, DiPalma, and
Quezada
Date Introduced: March 04, 2026
Referred To: Senate Finance
1 WHEREAS, Public transportation is a lifeline for thousands of Rhode Islanders. It builds
2 thriving communities, creates jobs, eases traffic congestion and promotes a cleaner environment;
3 and
4 WHEREAS, In myriad areas across the State, Rhode Island's workforce is severely
5 limited and understaffed. To begin to rectify this growing challenge, initiatives must be instituted
6 to counter the downward trend; and
7 WHEREAS, An appropriation of five million dollars ($5,000,000) is essential in order to
8 support RIPTA's hiring and training of more bus operators by increasing wages and benefits to be
9 competitive and in line with the rising cost of living in Rhode Island and with our neighboring
10 states, and to keep services running at the current levels to get Rhode Islanders where they need
11 to go; now, therefore be it
12 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated, out of money in the treasury not
13 otherwise appropriated for the fiscal year 2026-2027, the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000)
14 to the Rhode Island Public Transit Authority to continue current service throughout the State, hire
15 and train more bus operators, and implement the State's Act on Climate goals; and the state
16 controller is hereby authorized and directed to draw their orders upon the general treasurer for the
17 payment of said sum, or so much thereof as may be from time to time required, upon receipt

1 of properly authenticated vouchers.
