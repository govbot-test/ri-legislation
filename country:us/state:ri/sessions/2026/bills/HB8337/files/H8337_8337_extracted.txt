4 (a) Rate reform.
5 (1) The rates to be paid by the state to nursing facilities licensed pursuant to chapter 17 of

10 promulgate or modify the principles of reimbursement for nursing facilities in effect as of July 1,

12 of the Social Security Act.

18 developed by the executive office:
19 (i) A direct-care rate adjusted for resident acuity;

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO HUMAN SERVICES -- MEDICAL ASSISTANCE
***
1 This act would require the executive office of health and human services to consider a new
2 element when reviewing the appropriate Medicaid payments to be paid to nursing facilities. The
3 act would require the executive office to specifically review the increases in direct and indirect
4 costs beginning October of 2027 to reflect changes using data from the most recent finalized year
5 of a facility's costs.
6 This act would take effect upon passage.
