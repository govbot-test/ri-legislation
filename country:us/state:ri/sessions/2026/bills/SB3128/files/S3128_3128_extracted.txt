A N A C T
RELATING TO FOOD AND DRUGS -- THE RHODE ISLAND CANNABIS ACT
Introduced By: Senator Louis P. DiPalma
Date Introduced: March 20, 2026
Referred To: Senate Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 21-28.11 of the General Laws entitled "The Rhode Island Cannabis
2 Act" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 21-28.11-31.1. Cannabis safety -- Responsible usage.
4 (a) Annually, the cannabis control commission shall appropriate the sum of two hundred
5 thousand dollars ($200,000) to the substance use mental health leadership council (the
6 “SUMHLC”) to address hazardous and underage cannabis use, including alternatives, education,
7 and environmental strategies.
8 (b) Annually, the cannabis control commission shall appropriate the sum of five hundred
9 thousand dollars ($500,000) to the SUMHLC for a public facing web and social media presence
10 and ongoing media campaigns.
11 (c) Annually, by October 1, the SUMHLC shall report on the usage of each appropriation
12 grant to the president of the senate and speaker of the house.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
