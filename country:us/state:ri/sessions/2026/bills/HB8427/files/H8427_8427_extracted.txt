A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES AND CARRIERS -- REGULATORY POWERS OF
ADMINISTRATION
Introduced By: Representatives Hull, J. Lombardi, Fogarty, Slater, and Sanchez
Date Introduced: April 10, 2026
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 39-3 of the General Laws entitled "Regulatory Powers of
2 Administration" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 39-3-37.4. Informational notice on gas bills -- Gas distribution company.
4 (a) Every gas distribution company that shall charge for the distribution of natural gas to
5 any house, building, tenement, or estate shall conspicuously display upon the bill or statement for
6 any customer the following information:
7 (1) The total number of therms consumed;
8 (2) The total cost of distributing the gas to the customer;
9 (3) The customer charge;
10 (4) Conservation costs;
11 (5) All applicable credits;
12 (6) Charges that support low-income gas customers;
13 (7) Applicable taxes; and
14 (8) All other costs, charges, or fees added to the bill or statement.
15 (b) The gas distribution company shall issue a single bill for gas service to all customers in
16 its service territory.
17 (c) For purposes of this section, "conspicuously display" means including a visual
18 illustration showing each component of the utility bill and the cost associated with each component

1 including, but not limited to, supply, distribution, ratepayer funded programs and policy, taxes and
2 all other costs. The division shall have the authority to approve or deny the adequacy of the format
3 chosen by the company for compliance with this provision.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
