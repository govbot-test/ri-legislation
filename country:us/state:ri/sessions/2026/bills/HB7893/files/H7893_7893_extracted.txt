JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- OFFENSES PERTAINING TO SCHOOLS
Introduced By: Representatives Morales, Sanchez, Giraldo, Cruz, Potter, and Cotter
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Education
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 16-38 of the General Laws entitled "Offenses Pertaining to Schools
2 [See Title 16 Chapter 97 — The Rhode Island Board of Education Act]" is hereby amended by
3 adding thereto the following section:
4 16-38-15. Legacy admissions.
5 (a) No public or private university shall extend preferential treatment to an applicant in any
6 manner because of the student's familial relationship to a previous attendee during admissions
7 consideration.
8 (b) No public or private university shall inquire about a prospective student's familial
9 relationship to a previous attendee on an application for admission. If the university learns about
10 the prospective student's familial relationship to a previous attendee during the application process,
11 the institution shall not extend preferential treatment to the prospective student.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
