JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
PROCLAIMING SEPTEMBER 14 TO SEPTEMBER 18, 2026, TO BE NATIONAL ADULT
EDUCATION AND FAMILY LITERACY WEEK IN THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Senator Lori Urso
Date Introduced: June 04, 2026
Referred To: Placed on the Senate Consent Calendar
1 WHEREAS, More than 43 million Americans cannot read or write at the most basic
2 level, and 62.7 million people lack basic math skills and the credentials necessary to succeed in
3 post-secondary education; and
4 WHEREAS, One in three young adults in the United States does not complete High
5 School; and
6 WHEREAS, There is a critical shortage of skilled workers across the private sector, with
7 an estimated 7.5 million positions being unfilled; and
8 WHEREAS, Adult Education programs play a transformative role in helping Americans
9 achieve basic skills, gain credentials, and prepare for career and college opportunities; and
10 WHEREAS, Adult Education programs-offered through high schools, adult schools,
11 community colleges, and community centers-provide accessible, high-value instruction that
12 equips citizens with the skills needed to thrive personally and professionally; now, therefore be it
13 RESOLVED, That this Senate of the State of Rhode Island hereby values and supports
14 lifelong learning as a cornerstone of individual opportunity, economic growth, and community
15 strength; and be it further
16 RESOLVED, That this Senate hereby recognizes the enduring importance of adult
17 education in transforming lives and empowering our citizens to achieve their full potential; and be
18 it further
19 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be hereby is authorized and directed to transmit

1 a duly certified copy of this resolution to the Commissioner of the Rhode Island Department of
2 Education.
