JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026

A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- LOW AND MODERATE INCOME HOUSING
Introduced By: Senators Felag, and DiPalma
Date Introduced: February 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
(by request)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-53 of the General Laws entitled "Low and Moderate Income
2 Housing" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-53-17. Tiverton affordable housing units.
4 In the town of Tiverton, at any time that there exists less than ten percent (10%) affordable
5 housing in accordance with the provisions of § 45-53-3.1, the minimum number of affordable
6 housing units to be built by any developer in the town pursuant to any approved plan, permit or
7 project shall comprise of at least fifty percent (50%), of the units or dwellings constituting or
8 consisting of affordable housing units until there exists full compliance with affordable housing
9 requirements of chapter 53 of title 45.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
