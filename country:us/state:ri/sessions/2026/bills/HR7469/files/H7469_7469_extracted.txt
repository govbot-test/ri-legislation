H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
PROCLAIMING FEBRUARY OF 2026 AS “AMERICAN HEART MONTH” IN THE STATE
OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Felix, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Chippendale, J.
Lombardi, Alzate, Tanzi, Hull, Stewart, and Santucci
Date Introduced: February 03, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, Every year, one in four Americans of all ages, genders, and ethnicities die
2 from heart disease; and
3 WHEREAS, In 2022, 22 percent of all deaths in Rhode Island were due to heart disease;
4 and
5 WHEREAS, The primary factors affecting heart disease are high cholesterol, high blood
6 pressure, smoking, diabetes, and excessive alcohol use; and
7 WHEREAS, American Heart Month 2026 focuses on “heart health and preventing
8 cardiovascular disease” and the need to nurture this vital organ to ensure enduring strength and
9 well-being; and
10 WHEREAS, To show support for American Heart Month, citizens are encouraged to
11 wear red and encourage others to join in the “colorful cause”; and
12 WHEREAS, In addition to wearing red, Rhode Islanders can use social media to raise
13 awareness and share heart health facts, learn CPR, start a fitness challenge, or volunteer their time
14 with organizations focusing on heart health; now, therefore be it
15 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
16 proclaims February of 2026 as “American Heart Month” and moreover supports all efforts to
17 raise awareness of heart disease; and be it further
18 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
19 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to the Director of the Rhode Island Department of

1 Health, Jerome Larkin, MD and the American Heart Association – Rhode Island.
