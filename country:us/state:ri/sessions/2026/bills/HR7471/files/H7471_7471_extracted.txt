H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
COMMEMORATING "NATIONAL SCHOOL COUNSELING WEEK" FROM FEBRUARY
2ND THROUGH FEBRUARY 6TH, 2026, IN THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Shekarchi, Blazejewski, McNamara, Chippendale,
Corvese, Furtado, Fascia, Sanchez, Tanzi, and Kennedy
Date Introduced: February 03, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, School counselors are employed in public and private schools to help
2 students reach their full potential; and
3 WHEREAS, School counselors are actively committed to helping students explore their
4 abilities, strengths, interests, and talents as these traits relate to career awareness and
5 development; and
6 WHEREAS, School counselors help parents focus on ways to further the educational,
7 personal and social growth of their children; and
8 WHEREAS, School counselors work with teachers and other educators to help students
9 explore their potential and set realistic goals for themselves; and
10 WHEREAS, School counselors seek to identify and utilize community resources that can
11 enhance and complement comprehensive school counseling programs and help students become
12 productive members of society; and
13 WHEREAS, Comprehensive developmental school counseling programs are considered
14 an integral part of the educational process that enables all students to achieve success in school;
15 now, therefore be it
16 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
17 commemorates February 2nd through 6th, 2026, as "National School Counseling Week"; and be
18 it further
19 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to

1 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Anne-Marie Flaherty, President, Rhode Island
2 School Counselor Association.
