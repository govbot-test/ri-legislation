A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- SPECIAL STOPS REQUIRED
Introduced By: Representatives Boylan, Fogarty, Casimiro, DeSimone, Read, Hull,
Spears, Dawson, Felix, and Kislak
Date Introduced: February 11, 2026
Referred To: House State Government & Elections
(by request)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 31-20 of the General Laws entitled "Special Stops Required" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 31-20-18. Posting bike path stop signs.
4 The department of transportation, in coordination with the traffic safety commission, shall
5 on all state highways, erect and maintain adequate signs requiring operators of motor vehicles to
6 come to a complete stop because of the existence of an intersection of a bike path, and roadway, as
7 defined in § 31-1-23.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
