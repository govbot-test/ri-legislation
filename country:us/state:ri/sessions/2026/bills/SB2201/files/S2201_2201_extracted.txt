S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO MILITARY AFFAIRS AND DEFENSE -- EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT
Introduced By: Senators Paolino, Thompson, Appollonio, Famiglietti, Patalano, Dimitri,
and Gu
Date Introduced: January 23, 2026
Referred To: Senate Commerce
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 30-15-3 of the General Laws in Chapter 30-15 entitled "Emergency
2 Management" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 30-15-3. Definitions.
4 As used in this chapter:
5 (1) “Bioterrorism” means the intentional use of any microorganism, virus, infectious
6 substance or biological product that may be engineered as a result of biotechnology or any naturally
7 occurring or bioengineered component of any such microorganism, virus, infectious substance, or
8 biological product, to cause death, disease, or other biological malfunction in a human, an animal,
9 a plant or other living organism;
10 (2) "Director" means the director of the Rhode Island emergency management agency;
11 (2)(3) “Disaster” means occurrence or imminent threat of widespread or severe damage,
12 injury, or loss of life or property resulting from any natural or man-made cause, including, but not
13 limited to:
14 (i) Fire;
15 (ii) Flood;
16 (iii) Earthquake;
17 (iv) Wind, storm, wave action, oil spill, or other water contamination requiring emergency
18 action to avert danger or damage;
19 (v) Volcanic activity;

1 (vi) Epidemic;
2 (vii) Air contamination;
3 (viii) Blight;
4 (ix) Drought;
5 (x) Infestation;
6 (xi) Explosion;
7 (xii) Riots;
8 (xiii) Hostile military or paramilitary action;
9 (xiv) Endangerment of the health, safety, or resources of the people of the state;
10 (xv) Acts of bioterrorism;
11 (3)(4) “Political subdivision” means any city or town in Rhode Island; and
12 (4)(5) “Unorganized militia” means all able-bodied persons between the ages of sixteen
13 (16) and fifty (50) years.
14 SECTION 2. Chapter 30-15 of the General Laws entitled "Emergency Management" is
15 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
16 30-15-5.1. Flood insurance.
17 The director and the commerce commission shall develop and implement a plan to assist
18 businesses in obtaining flood insurance through the National Flood Insurance program.
19 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO MILITARY AFFAIRS AND DEFENSE -- EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT
***
1 This act would require the director of the Rhode Island emergency management agency
2 and the Rhode Island commerce commission to develop and implement a plan to assist businesses
3 in obtaining flood insurance through the National Flood Insurance program.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
