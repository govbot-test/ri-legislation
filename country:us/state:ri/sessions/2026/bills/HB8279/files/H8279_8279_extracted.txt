A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- LOW AND MODERATE INCOME HOUSING
Introduced By: Representatives Costantino, and Santucci
Date Introduced: March 11, 2026
Referred To: House Municipal Government & Housing
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-53 of the General Laws entitled "Low and Moderate Income
2 Housing" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-53-17. Smithfield emergency moratorium.
4 (a) If the town of Smithfield, shall at any time have an aggregate of two hundred fifty (250)
5 or more units proposed in a comprehensive permit project or projects, the town of Smithfield may
6 enact an emergency moratorium and not accept or process further applications. No application
7 proposed, offered, submitted or tendered during any moratorium imposed pursuant to the
8 provisions of this section shall have any grandfathered status.
9 (b) The town of Smithfield, upon adoption of any moratorium in accordance with the
10 provisions of subsection (a) of this section, may continue to impose the moratorium until such time
11 as comprehensive permit requirements in § 45-53-4 are amended.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
