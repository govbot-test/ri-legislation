It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 31-38-7 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-38 entitled "Inspection
2 of Motor Vehicles" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 31-38-7. Operation of official stations.
4 (a) No permit for an official station shall be assigned or transferred or used at any location
5 other than designated in it, and the permit shall be posted in a conspicuous place at the designated
6 location.
7 (b) The state-certified person operating an official inspection station shall issue a certificate
8 of inspection and approval upon an official form to the owner of a vehicle upon inspection of the
9 vehicle and determining that its equipment required under the provisions of this chapter is in good
10 condition and proper adjustment, otherwise, no certificate shall be issued. A record and report shall
11 be made of every inspection and every certificate issued. The records shall be kept available for
12 review by those employees of the department of revenue that the director may designate.
13 (c) The following fees shall be charged for inspection and issuance of certificate of
14 inspection and approval:
15 (1) For every vehicle with a registered gross weight of not more than eight thousand five
16 hundred pounds (8,500 lbs.), the fee shall be included with the fee charged pursuant to § 31-47.1-
17 11;
18 (2) For every vehicle of a registered gross weight of more than eight thousand five hundred
19 pounds (8,500 lbs.) or more, except trailers, fifteen dollars ($15.00);

1 (3) For every motorcycle and electrically powered vehicle, eleven dollars ($11.00) fifteen
2 dollars ($15.00);
3 (4) For every trailer or semi-trailer with a registered gross weight of more than one
4 thousand pounds (1,000 lbs.), eleven dollars ($11.00); and
5 (5) Provided, that for the inspection of vehicles used for the transportation of persons for
6 hire, as provided in § 31-22-12, and subject to an inspection pursuant to chapter 47.1 of this title,
7 the fee shall be included with the fee charged pursuant to § 31-47.1-11.
8 (d) The director of the department of revenue may establish a state inspection facility at
9 which any motor vehicle may be reinspected at no cost to the owner. The state inspection facility
10 may inspect all public conveyance vehicles or these inspections may be otherwise provided for by
11 the director, or any other vehicles which, in the opinion of the director of revenue, or his or her
12 designee, require specific testing to ensure for the health and safety of the general public.
13 (e) Any other inspections or activities which may be required to be performed at a state
14 inspection facility may be performed at any official inspection station if determined by the director.
15 SECTION 2. Section 31-47.1-11 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-47.1 entitled "Motor
16 Vehicle Emissions Inspection Program" is hereby amended to read as follows:
17 31-47.1-11. Fees.
18 (a) Beginning in fiscal year 2015 until January 1, 2027, a fee of fifty-five dollars ($55.00)
19 is to be charged for each motor vehicle inspected. The amount of fees collected shall provide for
20 the cost of the inspection, the costs of administering the motor vehicle emissions inspection
21 program and other costs provided by law. The fee must be paid for each motor vehicle inspected at
22 an emissions inspection station at the time of the inspection and is payable whether a compliance
23 certificate, waiver certificate, or no certificate is issued. There shall be no fee charged for one
24 reinspection of a vehicle that failed an initial inspection when the reinspection is conducted at the
25 AIRS that conducted the initial inspection.
26 Of the fifty-five dollars ($55.00) fee, nineteen dollars ($19.00) shall be retained by the
27 inspection station owner to cover the costs of performing the inspection. The remaining thirty-six
28 dollars ($36.00) shall be remitted to the program manager. The program manager shall retain no
29 more than four dollars ($4.00) of the fee and remit no less than thirty-two dollars ($32.00) for
30 deposit in the Rhode Island highway maintenance account. Be it further provided that twenty
31 dollars ($20.00) generated from the fee be deposited into the Rhode Island highway maintenance
32 fund according to the schedule provided in subsection (b) of § 39-18.1-5. The general assembly
33 shall annually appropriate such sums as may be required to cover the costs of administering the
34 program by the division of motor vehicles and the department of environmental management.

2 including motorcycle inspections, with an allocation of ten dollars ($10.00) to the Rhode Island

4 This act would take effect on January 1, 2027.
