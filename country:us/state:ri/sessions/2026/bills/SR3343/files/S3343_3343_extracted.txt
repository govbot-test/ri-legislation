S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING THE REPORTING AND EXPIRATION DATES OF THE SPECIAL
LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY AND PROVIDE RECOMMENDATIONS FOR
POTENTIAL CHANGES IN LEGISLATION/OVERSIGHT OF THE ACT ON CLIMATE
Introduced By: Senator Samuel D. Zurier
Date Introduced: June 02, 2026
Referred To: Placed on the Senate Consent Calendar
1 RESOLVED, That the special legislative commission created by resolution No. 313
2 passed by the Senate at its January session, A.D. 2025, and approved May 15, 2025, entitled
3 "Senate Resolution Creating a Special Legislative Commission To Study and Provide
4 Recommendations for Potential Changes in Legislation/Oversight of The Act on Climate" is
5 hereby authorized to continue its study and make a report to the Senate on or before May 1, 2027,
6 and said commission shall expire on June 1, 2027; and be it further
7 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting authorized by resolution No. 313 passed by the
8 Senate at its January session, A.D. 2025, and approved May 15, 2025, be and the same is hereby
9 rescinded.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N

1 This resolution would extend the reporting and expiration dates of the special legislative
2 commission to study and provide recommendations for potential changes in legislation/oversight
3 of the Act on Climate from May 1, 2026, to May 1, 2027, and said commission would expire on
4 June 1, 2027.
