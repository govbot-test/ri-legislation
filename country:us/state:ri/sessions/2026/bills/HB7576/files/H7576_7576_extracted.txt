LC004897

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
Introduced By: Representatives Kislak, Potter, Serpa, Cortvriend, McGaw, Boylan,
Caldwell, Cotter, Spears, and Carson
Date Introduced: February 06, 2026
Referred To: House State Government & Elections
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 38-2 of the General Laws entitled "Access to Public Records" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 38-2-17. Traffic accidents and improvement data.
4 Notwithstanding any other provisions of this chapter or state law, any records that, under
5 23 U.S.C. § 407, are not discoverable or admissible in court proceedings or allowed to be
6 considered for other purposes in damage actions shall nonetheless be deemed public records;
7 provided, however, the records may not be used for purposes prohibited by that federal statute.
8 When making the record public, whether in response to a request pursuant to this chapter, or
9 otherwise, a public body may provide written notice of the aforementioned restrictions on their use.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC004897
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC RECORDS -- ACCESS TO PUBLIC RECORDS
***
1 This act would make traffic accident data a public record, subject to federal law.
2 This act would take effect upon passage.
