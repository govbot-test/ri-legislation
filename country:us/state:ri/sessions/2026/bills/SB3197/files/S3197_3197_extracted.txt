A N A C T
RELATING TO COURTS AND CIVIL PROCEDURE -- PROCEDURE GENERALLY --
CAUSE OF ACTION
Introduced By: Senators Dimitri, Patalano, LaMountain, Famiglietti, Appollonio, and
Thompson
Date Introduced: April 03, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 9-1-50 of the General Laws in Chapter 9-1 entitled "Causes of
2 Action" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 9-1-50. Settled claims not paid within thirty (30) days.
4 (a) Whenever any claim is settled, the insurance company, adjusting company, or any other
5 person, firm, or corporation responsible for paying the settlement shall make payment within thirty
6 (30) days from the date the claimant or the claimant’s attorney sends the release. The signed release
7 may be transmitted to the party responsible for paying the settlement or their attorney in electronic
8 format. The signed release may be transmitted to the party responsible for paying the settlement or
9 their attorney in electronic format. Failure to make payment within thirty (30) days shall raise a
10 presumption that failure to do so was a willful and wanton disregard for the rights of the claimant.
11 In addition to all other remedies, the payor shall be liable to the claimant in a separate cause of
12 action for punitive damages and interest which shall be computed at the rate of twelve percent
13 (12%) per annum from the date the cause of action giving rise to the settlement occurred until the
14 judgment on the claim brought pursuant to this section is entered.
15 (b) Any claim brought under the provisions of this section shall be given a priority on the
16 trial calendar.

1 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
