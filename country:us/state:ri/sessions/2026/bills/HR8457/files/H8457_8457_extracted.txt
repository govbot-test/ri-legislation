S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
PROCLAIMING APRIL TO BE "HOLOCAUST & GENOCIDE AWARENESS MONTH" IN
THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Kislak, Kazarian, Knight, Boylan, Shekarchi,
Blazejewski, Chippendale, Biah, Finkelman, and Ackerman
Date Introduced: April 16, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, The State of Rhode Island and the Rhode Island General Assembly passed
2 legislation in 2016 requiring Holocaust and genocide education for secondary public education;
3 and
4 WHEREAS, The State of Rhode Island passed legislation establishing the RI Holocaust
5 and Genocide Education Commission to oversee the implementation of Holocaust and genocide
6 education in the state’s secondary schools with the hopes of better understanding how the
7 Holocaust and genocides can be averted in the future; and
8 WHEREAS, Each year, the RI House of Representatives overwhelmingly passes
9 resolutions remembering the Holocaust, Armenian Genocide and other genocides, recognizing the
10 importance of “Never Again”; and
11 WHEREAS, It is essential to our State and our Nation that we educate our youth about
12 the millions of men, women, and children who have and continue to be slaughtered and
13 ostracized simply because of their beliefs and their heritage, or their strength or frailty, in order to
14 not let this brutality and senseless hatred and carnage continue; now, therefore be it
15 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
16 proclaims April to be “Holocaust & Genocide Awareness Month” and strongly encourages Rhode
17 Island’s history and social science educators to incorporate lessons on past and current genocide
18 atrocities into their curriculum and classroom discussions in order to encourage and foster a new
19 generation with insight and the pledge to work to eradicate bigotry, prejudice, brutality, and

1 injustice; and be it further
2 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
3 transmit duly certified copies of this resolution to Angélica Infante-Green, Commissioner of the
4 Rhode Island Department of Elementary and Secondary Education and the Rhode Island
5 Holocaust and Genocide Education Commission.
