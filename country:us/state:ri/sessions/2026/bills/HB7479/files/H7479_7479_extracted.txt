7 association, corporation or trust, resident or nonresident, who in whole or in part offers for sale,
8 sells or distributes any new motor vehicle to new motor vehicle dealers is a common entity of the

13 lawfully carried on in accordance with the terms of all applicable building codes, zoning, and other
14 land-use regulatory ordinances.
15 (4)(5) “Factory branch” means a branch office maintained by a manufacturer for the
16 purpose of selling, or offering for sale, vehicles to a distributor or new motor vehicle dealer, or for

18 (5)(6) “Franchise” means the agreement or contract between any new motor vehicle
19 manufacturer, written or otherwise, and any new motor vehicle dealer which purports to fix the
20 legal rights and liabilities of the parties to that agreement or contract, and pursuant to which the
21 dealer purchases and resells the franchise product or leases or rents the dealership premises.
22 (6)(7) “Fraud” includes, in addition to its normal legal connotation, the following: a
23 misrepresentation in any manner, whether intentionally false or due to gross negligence, of a
24 material fact; a promise or representation not made honestly and in good faith; and an intentional
25 failure to disclose a material fact.
26 (7)(8) “Good faith” means honesty in fact and the observation of reasonable commercial
27 standards of fair dealing in the trade as defined and interpreted in § 6A-2-103(1)(b).
28 (8)(9) “Manufacturer” means any person, partnership, firm, association, corporation, or
