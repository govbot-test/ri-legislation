A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- SCHOOL COMMITTEES AND SUPERINTENDENTS
Introduced By: Representatives Biah, Read, Speakman, Chippendale, Paplauskas, Potter,
Cruz, McNamara, J. Lombardi, and Hull
Date Introduced: March 20, 2026
Referred To: House Education
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 16-2 of the General Laws entitled "School Committees and
2 Superintendents [See Title 16 Chapter 97 — The Rhode Island Board of Education Act]" is hereby
3 amended by adding thereto the following section:
4 16-2-3.1. School calendar flexibility during declared states of emergency.
5 (a) Notwithstanding any general or special law, rule, or regulation requiring a minimum
6 number of school days or instructional hours for public schools in the state, any day or days during
7 which a public school is closed due to a state of emergency declared by the governor of the State
8 of Rhode Island shall be deemed excused for purposes of compliance with the minimum school
9 days calendar requirements,
10 (b) School districts and public schools shall not be required to submit individual waiver
11 requests to the Rhode Island department of elementary and secondary education for instructional
12 days missed as a result of a declared state of emergency.
13 (c) Any school day canceled due to a declared state of emergency shall not be required to
14 be made up by extending the school year, adding additional school days, or otherwise modifying
15 the approved school calendar.
16 (d) The commissioner of elementary and secondary education may issue general guidance
17 to school districts regarding emergency closures, but shall not require individual waivers for
18 closures occurring during the officially declared state of emergency period.
19 (e) The provisions of this section shall apply only to school closures resulting directly from

1 a natural disaster, severe weather event, public safety emergency, or other emergency conditions
2 for which the governor has issued an official state of emergency declaration.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
