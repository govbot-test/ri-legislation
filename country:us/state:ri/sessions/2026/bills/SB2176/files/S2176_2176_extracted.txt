JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026

A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES
Introduced By: Senator Alana DiMario
Date Introduced: January 16, 2026
Referred To: Senate Special Legislation and Veterans Affairs
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 31-3 of the General Laws entitled "Registration of Vehicles" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 31-3-132. Special plate for the Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers.
4 (a) The administrator of the division of motor vehicles is empowered to make available
5 special motor vehicle registration plates for the not-for-profit entity Friends of the Historic
6 Narragansett Towers. The plates shall be designed to reference the not-for-profit entity Friends of
7 the Historic Narragansett Towers.
8 (b) The special plate shall be displayed upon the same registration number assigned to the
9 vehicle for which it was issued and shall be used in place of and in the same manner as the
10 registration plates issued to the vehicle. The original registration plates for the vehicle shall be
11 removed from the vehicle and returned to the division of motor vehicles. The registration certificate
12 for the plates shall be carried in the vehicle in accordance with § 31-3-9. The registration certificate
13 shall be in effect for the special plate.
14 (c) The Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers motor vehicle plates shall be the same
15 size as regular motor vehicle plates and shall be designed by the Friends of the Historic Narragansett
16 Towers in conjunction with the division of motor vehicles, with the design approved by the Rhode
17 Island state police.
18 (d) The Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers plates shall be subject to compliance
19 with § 31-3-117 relating to the number of minimum prepaid orders of plates per plate type (i.e.,

1 passenger, commercial, etc.) and any required payment. The Friends of the Historic Narragansett
2 Towers plates shall not be issued unless the minimum order requirements are met. The initial order
3 will be handled by the Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers and shall not be submitted to
4 the division of motor vehicles for production until the minimum order(s) have been met and the
5 proper paperwork submitted to the division.
6 (e) The administrator of motor vehicles shall develop prepayment procedures and any other
7 procedures deemed necessary to carry out the purposes of this section.
8 (f) In addition to the regular prescribed motor vehicle registration fee, Friends of the
9 Historic Narragansett Towers plates shall be subject to a forty dollar ($40.00) issuance surcharge.
10 (g) The forty dollar ($40.00) issuance surcharge shall be allocated as follows: twenty
11 dollars ($20.00) shall be allocated to the general fund and the remaining twenty dollars ($20.00)
12 shall be distributed annually to the Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers to host cultural and
13 performing arts programs to raise public awareness and support for preservation and use of the
14 Towers in Rhode Island.
15 (h) A ten dollar ($10.00) surcharge for subsequent registration renewals shall be allocated
16 to the Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers.
17 (i) In consideration of the receipt of funds from the registration of the Friends of the
18 Historic Narragansett Towers plates, the Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers must use any
19 Rhode Island-sourced funds in and for the benefit of Rhode Island-based charitable organizations.
20 (j) The Friends of the Historic Narragansett Towers will be required to submit an annual
21 accounting report before such monies are distributed.
22 (k) There shall be no refunds for early cancellation of the Friends of the Historic
23 Narragansett Towers plates.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
