JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- LOW AND MODERATE INCOME HOUSING
Introduced By: Senators Famiglietti, Dimitri, and Tikoian
Date Introduced: March 05, 2026
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-53 of the General Laws entitled "Low and Moderate Income
2 Housing" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-53-17. North Providence emergency moratorium.
4 (a) If the town of North Providence, shall at any time have an aggregate of two hundred
5 fifty (250) or more units proposed in a comprehensive permit project or projects, the town of North
6 Providence may enact an emergency moratorium and not accept or process further applications. No
7 application proposed, offered, submitted or tendered during any moratorium imposed pursuant to
8 the provisions of this section shall have any grandfathered status.
9 (b) The town of North Providence, upon adoption of any moratorium in accordance with
10 the provisions of subsection (a) of this section, may continue to impose the moratorium until such
11 time as comprehensive permit requirements in § 45-53-4 are amended.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
