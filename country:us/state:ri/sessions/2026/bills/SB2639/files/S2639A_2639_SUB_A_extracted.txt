A N A C T
RELATING TO FOOD AND DRUGS -- SINGLE-USE PLASTIC STRAWS
Introduced By: Senators Dimitri, Famiglietti, Tikoian, Britto, Thompson, and Patalano
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: Senate Commerce
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 21-27.2-2 of the General Laws in Chapter 21-27.2 entitled "Single-
2 Use Plastic Straws" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 21-27.2-2. Prohibited practices exception.
4 A food service establishment shall not provide a single-use plastic straw to a consumer
5 unless requested or by the consumer or is directly taken from a single straw dispenser.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
