JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
Introduced By: Representatives Caldwell, Craven, Dawson, and O'Brien
Date Introduced: June 10, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any other general or special law to the contrary, Meghan
2 Elizabeth McEntee may join Taylor Lauren Criscuolo and Michael Joseph McEntee Jr., in marriage
3 within the Town of Narragansett, Rhode Island on or about September 25, 2026. Meghan Elizabeth
4 McEntee is hereby authorized and empowered to join the foregoing persons in marriage pursuant
5 to and in accordance with Chapter 3 of Title 15 of the General laws, entitled “Solemnization of
6 Marriages.”
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
