S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO ELECTIONS -- CONDUCT OF ELECTION AND VOTING EQUIPMENT,
AND SUPPLIES
Introduced By: Senators LaMountain, Dimitri, Burke, Felag, McKenney, and Bissaillon
Date Introduced: March 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
(Board of Elections)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 17-19-37 of the General Laws in Chapter 17-19 entitled "Conduct of
2 Election and Voting Equipment, and Supplies" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 17-19-37. Tabulation of state returns — Certificate of election. Tabulation of state
4 and federal returns – Certificate of election.
5 The state board shall proceed to tabulate the state and federal election returns and declare
6 the results as provided in chapter 22 of this title, and any candidate at the election challenging the
7 result as applied to him or her may petition the board within seven (7) nine (9) days after the
8 declaration to conduct a recount of the votes cast in the candidate’s election according to the
9 provisions set forth in § 17-19-37.1. After the completion of the recount, and as soon as it can do
10 so consistent with its other duties, the state board shall issue a certificate of election to the state and
11 federal candidate it determines to have been elected.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO ELECTIONS -- CONDUCT OF ELECTION AND VOTING EQUIPMENT,
AND SUPPLIES
***
1 This act would increase from seven (7) days to nine (9) days the period of time in which a
2 request for a recount of the votes cast can be made to the board of elections. No certificates of
3 election may be issued, until the recount if any is completed.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
