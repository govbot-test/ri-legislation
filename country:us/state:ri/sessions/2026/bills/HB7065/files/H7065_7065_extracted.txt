JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO HEALTH AND SAFETY -- TRUTH IN FOOD DISCLOSURE LAW
Introduced By: Representatives Perez, Fellela, Corvese, Ackerman, Sanchez, Felix, Hull,
Chippendale, Hopkins, and Fascia
Date Introduced: January 14, 2026
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 23-62 of the General Laws entitled "Truth in Food Disclosure Law"
2 is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 23-62-3. Reduction in added sugar in liquid beverages.
4 On and after January 1, 2030, all liquid beverages sold for consumption in Rhode Island
5 shall reduce the total amount of added sugars in the liquid beverage by fifty percent (50%) of the
6 added sugars content in that beverage as of July 1, 2026. As used herein, the term "added sugars"
7 has the same meaning as defined by the Food and Drug Administration rules on food labeling (21
8 CFR Part 101); provided, the amount of added sugars content shall be listed on the liquid beverage
9 container as provided by state and federal law.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect January 1, 2030.
