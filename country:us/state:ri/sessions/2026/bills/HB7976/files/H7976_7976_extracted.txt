A N A C T
RELATING TO GENERAL ASSEMBLY -- PERMANENT JOINT COMMITTEE ON
NAMING ALL NEW BUILDINGS, BRIDGES, EDIFICES AND OTHER STATE
CONSTRUCTIONS
Introduced By: Representatives Fogarty, Tanzi, Spears, McEntee, and Stewart
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 22-7.4 of the General Laws entitled "Permanent Joint Committee on
2 Naming All New Buildings, Bridges, Edifices and Other State Constructions" is hereby amended
3 by adding thereto the following section:
4 22-7.4-160. Mark S. Cruise '81 '84 Football Practice Field.
5 The University of Rhode Island football practice field, located at 5E West Alumni Avenue,
6 Kingston, RI 02881, shall hereafter be named and known as "Mark S. Cruise '81 '84 Football
7 Practice Field."
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
