A N A C T
RELATING TO MILITARY AFFAIRS AND DEFENSE -- EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT
Introduced By: Senator Linda L. Ujifusa
Date Introduced: February 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Finance
(by request)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 30-15 of the General Laws entitled "Emergency Management" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 30-15-46. Funding to support mutual aid control centers.
4 (a) The general assembly shall make an annual appropriation of not less than eighty
5 thousand dollars ($80,000) to the Rhode Island emergency management agency in the executive
6 department and created pursuant to the provisions of §30-15-5, to assist with emergency
7 communications during major or multiple emergency incidents or when a community has
8 exhausted its emergency resources and has requested assistance by the control centers under their
9 mutual aid agreement.
10 (b) These funds shall be distributed annually as follows:
11 (1) An amount of not less than twelve thousand five hundred dollars ($12,500) to the
12 communities designated as metro control, east bay control, northern control, and southern control;
13 and
14 (2) An amount of not less than seven thousand five hundred dollars ($7,500) to the
15 communities designated as back-up control centers.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
