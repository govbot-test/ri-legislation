JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION OF STATE EDUCATION AID TO THE TOWN OF
RICHMOND IN THE AMOUNT OF $331,064
Introduced By: Representative Megan L. Cotter
Date Introduced: April 17, 2026
Referred To: House Finance
1 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated to the TOWN OF RICHMOND
2 (hereinafter “Richmond”), which is part of the Chariho Regional School District, the sum of three
3 hundred thirty-one thousand, sixty-four dollars ($331,064), out of any monies in the treasury not
4 otherwise appropriated for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, to support and restore state
5 education aid to Richmond, and which sum is the amount by which total state education aid to
6 Richmond is proposed to be reduced by the Governor in his budget for the upcoming fiscal year;
7 and the state controller is hereby authorized and directed to draw orders upon the general
8 treasurer for the payment of said sum, or so much thereof as may be from time to time required,
9 upon receipt of properly authenticated vouchers.
