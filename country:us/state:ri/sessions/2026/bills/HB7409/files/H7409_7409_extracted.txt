A N A C T
RELATING TO COMMERCIAL LAW -- GENERAL REGULATORY PROVISIONS --
DECEPTIVE TRADE PRACTICES
Introduced By: Representatives Bennett, Edwards, McNamara, Kazarian, Corvese, Hull,
Noret, Shanley, Read, and Slater
Date Introduced: January 30, 2026
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 6-13.1 of the General Laws entitled "Deceptive Trade Practices" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 6-13.1-31. Credit card surcharge prohibited.
4 It shall be a deceptive trade practice in violation of this chapter for any retail establishment
5 offering goods or services for sale to impose a surcharge fee on a customer who uses a credit card
6 for a transaction occurring in the state.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
