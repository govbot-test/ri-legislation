S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO BUSINESSES AND PROFESSIONS -- PROTECTIONS OF HEALTHCARE
PROVIDERS ACT
Introduced By: Senators Lauria, Lawson, Euer, Valverde, Kallman, Mack, DiMario,
Murray, Pearson, and Bissaillon
Date Introduced: January 09, 2026
Referred To: Senate Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 5-37.8 of the General Laws entitled "Protections for Healthcare
2 Providers Act" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 5-37.8-4. Prescriptive protections for providers of legal protected healthcare activity.
4 (a) Notwithstanding any other provision in this chapter to the contrary, at the prescriber's
5 request, the prescription label for medication abortion prescription drugs may include the name of
6 the prescriber's healthcare practice instead of the name of the prescriber.
7 (b) For purposes of this section, “medication abortion prescription drugs” means
8 substances used in the course of medical treatment intended to induce the termination of a
9 pregnancy including, but not limited to, mifepristone and misoprostol.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
