S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
PARKING SPACES
Introduced By: Representatives Fellela, Fascia, Hull, Messier, and Shallcross Smith
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Municipal Government & Housing
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-53 of the General Laws entitled "Low and Moderate Income
2 Housing" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-53-17. Johnston low- or moderate-income housing parking spaces.
4 The town of Johnston may require two (2) parking spaces for each studio, one-bedroom,
5 or two (2) bedroom low- or moderate-income housing unit upon the municipality's determination
6 that there is limited or no local public transportation or off-street parking availability within a
7 reasonable proximity to these low- or moderate-income housing units.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- LOW AND MODERATE INCOME HOUSING--
PARKING SPACES
***
1 This act would permit Johnston to require two (2) parking spaces for each studio, one-
2 bedroom, or two-bedroom low- or moderate-income housing unit upon the determination that
3 limited or no local public transportation or off-street parking is availabile near these units.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
