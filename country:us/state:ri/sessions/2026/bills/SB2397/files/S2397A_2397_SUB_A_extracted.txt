5 4-13-42. Care of dogs.
6 (a) It shall be a violation of this section for an owner or keeper to:
7 (1) Keep any dog on a permanent tether that restricts movement of the tethered dog to an

10 (2) Tether a dog with a choke-type collar, head collar, or prong-type collar. The weight of

21 temperature is beyond the industry standard for the weather safety scale as set forth in the most

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO ANIMALS AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY -- DOGS
***
1 This act would increase penalties for a second or subsequent offense with a fine of not less
2 than two hundred fifty dollars ($250) or no more than one thousand dollars ($1,000) and up to
3 eleven (11) months imprisonment, for violating the law on the care of dogs. This act would further
4 amend existing law to include city and town animal control officers as agents authorized to enforce
5 the provisions of this law.
6 This act would take effect upon passage.
