JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES
Introduced By: Representatives Newberry, and Noret
Date Introduced: January 14, 2026
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 31-3-72 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-3 entitled "Registration
2 of Vehicles" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 31-3-72. Special plate for Mr. Potato Head.
4 (a) The administrator of the division of motor vehicles is empowered to make available a
5 special motor vehicle registration plate commemorating the fiftieth (50th) anniversary of Mr. Potato
6 Head. The plate shall bear the designation “Mr. Potato Head — 50th anniversary” and shall bear
7 the seal of the state imprinted on it. The special plate shall be displayed upon the same registration
8 number assigned to the vehicle for which it was issued and shall be used in place of and in the same
9 manner as the original registration plates issued to the vehicle. The original registration plates for
10 the vehicle shall be removed from the vehicle and the registration certificate for the plates shall be
11 carried in the vehicle, in accordance with § 31-3-9. The registration certificate shall be in effect for
12 the special plate.
13 (b) A special automobile or commercial commemorative Mr. Potato Head — 50th
14 anniversary plate shall be issued upon application using forms furnished by the administrator upon
15 payment in addition to the regular prescribed motor vehicle registration fee, and a service charge
16 of forty dollars ($40.00) for each issue. The service charge shall be paid to the administrator prior
17 to the administrator’s acceptance of the application. Twenty dollars ($20.00) of the service charge
18 shall be paid to the Rhode Island Community Food Bank and twenty dollars ($20.00) shall be
19 allocated to the division of motor vehicles for its costs in manufacturing and distributing the special

1 plates.
2 (c) Any current registration for “Mr. Potato Head- 50th anniversary" plates may remain as
3 an authorized and permitted registration provided the registration remains continuously renewed.
4 Any lapse, nonrenewal, revocation or loss or destruction of the plate or the registration for the plates
5 shall result in the permanent nonregistration and nonrenewal of registration for the plates. The “Mr.
6 Potato Head- 50th anniversary” plates shall not be transferred or registered to any other or
7 subsequent vehicle of the registered owner or the registered owner’s family or relatives.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
