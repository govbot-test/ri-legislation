A N A C T
RELATING TO INSURANCE -- ACCIDENT AND SICKNESS INSURANCE POLICIES
Introduced By: Senators Urso, Murray, Ujifusa, Vargas, Lauria, Valverde, Appollonio,
and Rogers
Date Introduced: March 04, 2026
Referred To: Senate Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 27-18 of the General Laws entitled "Accident and Sickness Insurance
2 Policies" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 27-18-96. Coverage for hormonal and non-hormonal therapy to treat the symptoms
4 of peri-menopause and menopause.
5 (a) Every individual or group health insurance contract, plan, or policy delivered, issued
6 for delivery, or renewed in this state on or after January 1, 2027, shall provide coverage for
7 medically necessary hormone therapy treatment to treat the symptoms of peri-menopause and
8 menopause.
9 (b) Coverage for hormone replacement therapy to treat the symptoms of peri-menopause
10 and menopause under this section shall mean at least one federal Food and Drug Administration-
11 approved hormonal and non-hormonal treatment, and at least one hormonal replacement therapy
12 option in each of the following modalities: oral, transdermal, topical, and vaginal.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO INSURANCE -- ACCIDENT AND SICKNESS INSURANCE POLICIES
***
1 This act would mandate health insurance coverage for medically necessary hormonal and
2 non-hormonal therapy to treat symptoms of peri-menopause and menopause, if the therapy is
3 recommended by a qualified healthcare provider who is licensed, accredited, or certified under
4 Rhode Island law and the therapy has been proven safe and effective. This coverage would be
5 mandated for all insurance policies issued on or after January 1, 2027.
6 This act would take effect upon passage.
