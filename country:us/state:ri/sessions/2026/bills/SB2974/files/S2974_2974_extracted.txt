A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- RIGHT-OF-WAY
Introduced By: Senators LaMountain, Felag, and Burke
Date Introduced: March 04, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
(Judiciary)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 31-17-5.1 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-17 entitled "Right-Of-
2 Way" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 31-17-5.1. Penalties.
4 Any person who violates any provision of §§ 31-17-1 — 31-17-5, pertaining to the right-
5 of-way of pedestrians, upon conviction, shall be sentenced to pay a fine of not more than seventy-
6 five ($75.00) subject to the fines enumerated in § 31.41.1-4.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

OF
