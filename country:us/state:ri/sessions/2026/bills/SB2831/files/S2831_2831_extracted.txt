S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO HEALTH AND SAFETY -- PRIMARY CARE TRAINING SITES PROGRAM
Introduced By: Senators Lauria, DiMario, Valverde, Murray, Vargas, Ujifusa, DiPalma,
Urso, Pearson, and LaMountain
Date Introduced: March 04, 2026
Referred To: Senate Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 23-17.30 of the General Laws entitled "Primary Care Training Sites
2 Program" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 23-17.30-8. Appropriation of funds.
4 (a) For the fiscal year 2026 and thereafter, the State of Rhode Island shall appropriate the
5 sum of two million seven hundred thousand dollars ($2,700,000) to the primary care training sites
6 program for the purpose of supporting its training for physicians, nurse practitioners and physician
7 assistants, established pursuant to the provisions of this chapter.
8 (b) The funds appropriated under this section shall be administered by the department of
9 health.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
