A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
Introduced By: Representative Carol Hagan McEntee
Date Introduced: May 29, 2026
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any other general or special law to the contrary, Noah David
2 Lloyd may join Lucia Helen Given and Noah Alexander Moroski in marriage within the Town of
3 Narragansett, Rhode Island, on or about September 27, 2026. Noah David Lloyd is hereby
4 authorized and empowered to join the foregoing persons in marriage pursuant to and in accordance
5 with Chapter 3 of Title 15 of the General Laws, entitled “Solemnization of marriages.”
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
