A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- ZONING ORDINANCES
Introduced By: Senators Bissaillon, Murray, Burke, and Britto
Date Introduced: February 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 `SECTION 1. Chapter 45-24 of the General Laws entitled "Zoning Ordinances" is hereby
2 amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-24-33.1. Recovery houses.
4 (a) For purposes of this section, a NARR-certified recovery residence means a residential
5 structure that provides recovery support services for those people recovering from substance use
6 addiction and that complies with standards established by the National Alliance for Recovery
7 Residences (NARR).
8 (b) Except as otherwise provided within the general laws, any law, regulation or ordinance
9 regulating the zoning and use of a residential NARR certified recovery residence:
10 (1) Shall treat a NARR-certified recovery residence as a residential use of property which
11 shall have all the rights and responsibilities afforded to a residential use of property.
12 (2) Shall not subject the recovery residence to any restrictions, prohibitions or other
13 provisions, including provisions related to the state building or fire codes that are not contained in
14 the Rhode Island life safety code § 26.1.1.1.8.3. At no time shall a recovery residence house under
15 this chapter be given the designation of a commercial structure for fire and or building codes.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
