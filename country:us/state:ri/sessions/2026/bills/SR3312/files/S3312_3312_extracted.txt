S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION OF $4,800,000 TO THE SUPPLEMENTAL NUTRITION
ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (SNAP) EAT WELL, BE WELL REWARDS PROGRAM
Introduced By: Senator Valarie J. Lawson
Date Introduced: May 22, 2026
Referred To: Senate Finance
1 WHEREAS, The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) Eat Well, Be Well
2 Rewards Program is the first statewide program of its kind in the country; and
3 WHEREAS, The Eat Well, Be Well Rewards Program provides approximately 90,000
4 Rhode Island households with up to $25 a month in additional benefits to purchase fruits and
5 vegetables; and
6 WHEREAS, The Eat Well, Be Well Rewards Program is a vital safety net for the
7 approximately 144,000 SNAP recipients struggling with food insecurity and access to healthy,
8 nutritious foods; and
9 WHEREAS, The Eat Well, Be Well Rewards Program increases the amount of nutritious
10 foods on the tables of our most vulnerable neighbors; now, therefore be it
11 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated, out of any money in the treasury not
12 otherwise appropriated for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum of four million eight
13 hundred thousand dollars ($4,800,000) to the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Eat
14 Well, Be Well Rewards Program; and the state controller is hereby authorized and directed to
15 draw their orders upon the general treasurer for the payment of said sum, upon receipt of properly
16 authenticated vouchers.
