LC003149

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
Introduced By: Representatives McNamara, Handy, and Fascia
Date Introduced: January 07, 2026
Referred To: House Finance
1 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated to the RHODE ISLAND AUDITORY
2 ORAL PROGRAM (hereinafter the “Program”), which is a statewide educational program
3 currently housed within the Foster School Department, the sum of one million, one hundred sixty-
4 seven thousand, one hundred dollars ($1,167,100), out of any monies in the treasury not
5 otherwise appropriated for fiscal year 2026-2027, to support the Program’s mission to provide
6 specialized auditory-oral programming for children with hearing loss who benefit from hearing
7 technology and to provide these children with tools necessary for successful inclusion and
8 achievement; and the state controller is hereby authorized and directed to draw orders upon the
9 general treasurer for the payment of said sum, or so much thereof as may be from time to time
10 required, upon receipt of properly authenticated vouchers.
========
LC003149
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE RHODE ISLAND AUDITORY ORAL PROGRAM
***
1 This joint resolution would authorize the appropriation of the sum of one million, one
2 hundred sixty-seven thousand, one hundred dollars ($1,167,100), to the Rhode Island Auditory
3 Oral Program, to support the program’s mission to provide specialized auditory-oral
4 programming for children with hearing loss.
