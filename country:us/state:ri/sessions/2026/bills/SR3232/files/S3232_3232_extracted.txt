S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
PROCLAIMING APRIL 28, 2026, TO BE "ARTS DAY IN RHODE ISLAND"
Introduced By: Senators Gallo, Tikoian, Ciccone, LaMountain, Valverde, McKenney,
Famiglietti, Appollonio, Murray, and Felag
Date Introduced: April 28, 2026
Referred To: Recommended for Immediate Consideration
1 WHEREAS, Arts and culture are embedded in every community, play a unique role in
2 the lives of our families, and contribute to livability, economic vitality, and enrichment of every
3 Rhode Islander; and
4 WHEREAS, Arts and culture are a source of strength, solace, and connection; and
5 WHEREAS, The U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis, reports that the arts sector
6 represented 3.3 percent of Rhode Island’s GDP—supporting nearly 18,000 jobs; and
7 WHEREAS, Our artists’ small businesses are a substantive component of economic
8 impact; and
9 WHEREAS, Because of the arts, event attendees have a ripple effect on neighboring
10 businesses, downtowns, and tourism – spending on average $38 in restaurants, shops, and other
11 local establishments in addition to buying an event ticket; and
12 WHEREAS, An 11-year study recently reported that during a school year, each student
13 enrolled in an arts course had an increase of a third of a day in attendance. This increase translates
14 to nine additional days of instruction – showing arts education can lead to improved academic
15 performance and school attendance; and
16 WHEREAS, The arts and culture embody much of the accumulated wisdom, intellect and
17 imagination of humankind; now, therefore be it
18 RESOLVED, That this Senate of the State of Rhode Island hereby proclaims April 28,
19 2026 to be "Arts Day in Rhode Island", and encourage all Rhode Islanders to reflect on how arts
20 and culture enrich their lives and recognize the critical role arts and culture play in our

1 communities; and be it further
2 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
3 transmit duly certified copies of this resolution to the Rhode Island State Council on the Arts
4 (RISCA) and the Rhode Island Coalition for the Arts.
