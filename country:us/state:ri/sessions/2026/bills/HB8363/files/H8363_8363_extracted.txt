S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- ZONING ORDINANCES
Introduced By: Representative Anthony J. DeSimone
Date Introduced: March 27, 2026
Referred To: House Municipal Government & Housing
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-24 of the General Laws entitled "Zoning Ordinances" is hereby
2 amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-24-78. Student housing licensing.
4 (a) Definitions. When used in this section, the following words and phrases shall be given
5 the following meanings:
6 (1) “Student” means an individual enrolled as an undergraduate or graduate student at any
7 state college as defined in § 16-17.1-1 as well as any private university or college within the state.
8 who commutes to a campus.
9 (2) “Student housing” means a dwelling unit inhabited exclusively by students.
10 (b) Authority.
11 (1) A municipality may, through ordinance, require private real property owners to obtain
12 a license to operate a dwelling unit as student housing. An ordinance adopted pursuant to this
13 section may include, but is not limited to:
14 (i) Annual or periodic licensing or registration requirements;
15 (ii) Inspection requirements;
16 (iii) Enforcement procedures; and
17 (iv) Administrative appeal procedures.
18 (c) Applicability.
19 (1) This section shall apply to dwelling units located within the boundaries of the

1 municipality enacting the ordinance.
2 (2) This section shall not apply to:
3 (i) Dormitories, residence halls, or other housing owned, operated, or directly controlled
4 by an educational institution;
5 (ii) Dwelling units owned or leased by an educational institution; and
6 (iii) Dwelling units where rents are regulated under a federal, state, or local housing
7 program limiting rent levels including, but not limited to, public housing or units rented through
8 subsidies, vouchers or waiver programs.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
