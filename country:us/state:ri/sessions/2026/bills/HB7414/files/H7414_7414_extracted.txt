____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- THE PAUL W. CROWLEY RHODE ISLAND STUDENT
INVESTMENT INITIATIVE
Introduced By: Representatives Slater, and O'Brien
Date Introduced: January 30, 2026
Referred To: House Education
(City of Providence)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 16-7.1 of the General Laws entitled "The Paul W. Crowley Rhode
2 Island Student Investment Initiative [See Title 16 Chapter 97 — The Rhode Island Board of
3 Education Act]" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
4 16-7.1-5.3. Extension of turnaround action plan terminated.
5 The extension of control issued by the department of elementary and secondary education
6 relative to the turnaround status of the Providence public school district (PPSD), issued on August
7 29, 2024, pursuant to § 16-7.1-5.1 extending the PPSD's turnaround action plan to October 15,
8 2027 is hereby null and void and terminated.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
