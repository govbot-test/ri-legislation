S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
PROCLAIMING APRIL 28, 2026, AS "PAY IT FORWARD WITH LOVE FROM TORI DAY"
IN THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Noret, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Finkelman,
Chippendale, Dawson, O'Brien, Phillips, Azzinaro, and Kennedy
Date Introduced: April 28, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, April 28 is recognized as “Global Pay It Forward Day,” a worldwide
2 movement dedicated to encouraging acts of kindness, generosity, and goodwill; and
3 WHEREAS, The concept of paying it forward inspires individuals to perform acts of
4 kindness without expectation of return, fostering a ripple effect that strengthens the social fabric
5 of our communities; and
6 WHEREAS, “Pay It Forward with Love from Tori” was established in loving memory of
7 Tori Lynn Andreozzi, whose life and spirit continue to inspire compassion, courage, and hope;
8 and
9 WHEREAS, This initiative encourages individuals of all ages to engage in meaningful
10 acts of kindness, grounded in the belief that nurturing empathy in our youth helps shape
11 compassionate adults and stronger communities; now, therefore be it
12 RESOLVED, That this House recognizes the profound impact that simple acts of
13 kindness can have in building a more inclusive, connected, and caring society; and be it further
14 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
15 proclaims April 28, 2026 as “Pay It Forward With Love From Tori Day” in the State of Rhode
16 Island and urges all citizens to recognize this day through acts of kindness, generosity, and
17 service to others, keeping alive the spirit that even the smallest gesture can make a lasting
18 difference: and be it further
19 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to

1 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Cathy Andreozzi.
