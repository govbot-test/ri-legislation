J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION OF $50,000 TO THE RHODE ISLAND DEPARTMENT OF
ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT (DEM) FOR NEW FORESTRY HOSES
Introduced By: Representatives Cotter, Chippendale, Kennedy, McGaw, Spears,
Casimiro, and Cruz
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Finance
1 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated, out of any money in the treasury not
2 otherwise appropriated for the fiscal year 2026-2027, the sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000)
3 to the Rhode Island Department of Environmental Management (DEM) for new forestry hoses;
4 and the state controller is hereby authorized and directed to draw their orders upon the general
5 treasurer for the payment of said sum, or so much thereof as may be from time to time required,
6 upon receipt of properly authenticated vouchers.
