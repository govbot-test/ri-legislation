A N A C T
RELATING TO ELECTIONS -- CONDUCT OF ELECTION AND VOTING EQUIPMENT,
AND SUPPLIES
Introduced By: Representatives J. Brien, Corvese, Lima, Chippendale, Hopkins, and
Newberry
Date Introduced: January 28, 2026
Referred To: House State Government & Elections
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 17-19 of the General Laws entitled "Conduct of Election and Voting
2 Equipment, and Supplies" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 17-19-24.4. Ranked choice voting prohibition.
4 (a) In all elections, including primary elections, for any local, state, or federal public office,
5 the following shall be prohibited:
6 (1) Voting systems that permit a voter to vote for more than one candidate for the same
7 office;
8 (2) Voting systems that permit a voter to rank multiple candidates for the same office; and
9 (3) Voting systems that reallocate a voter's vote for one candidate to another candidate for
10 the same office.
11 (b) Nothing in this section shall be construed to prevent a locality from holding at-large
12 elections or multi-member district elections, as long as voters have one vote per position and no
13 votes are transferred between candidates.
14 (c) Any existing or future ordinance by any political subdivision of this state which is in
15 conflict with this section is void.
16 (d) Any election for any public office in this state conducted under a voting system
17 prohibited by subsection (a) of this section is void, and no candidate shall be elected under such a
18 system.

1 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
