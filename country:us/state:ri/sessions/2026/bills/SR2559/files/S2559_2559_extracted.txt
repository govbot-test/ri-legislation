S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION OF $3,000,000 TO RHODE ISLAND LEGAL SERVICES,
INC.
Introduced By: Senators Bissaillon, LaMountain, McKenney, Britto, Dimitri, Mack, and
DiPalma
Date Introduced: February 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Finance
1 WHEREAS, Each year, thousands of hardworking low-income Rhode Island families,
2 victims of domestic violence, Veterans, and older residents depend on Rhode Island Legal
3 Services, Inc. for legal representation and assistance; and
4 WHEREAS, Rhode Island Legal Services, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) organization with statewide
5 operations; and
6 WHEREAS, In 2025, Rhode Island Legal Services, Inc. opened 5,805 cases and closed
7 4,713; and
8 WHEREAS, Rhode Island Legal Services, Inc. has prioritized helping Rhode Islanders
9 obtain safe, affordable housing, income maintenance and food, a healthy community, family
10 preservation and safety, financial stability, high-quality and appropriate education, and
11 employment and workers’ rights; and
12 WHEREAS, Since its founding, Rhode Island Legal Services, Inc. has helped over two
13 hundred thousand Rhode Islanders lead safe, productive, and self-sufficient lives; now, therefore
14 be it
15 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated, out of any money in the treasury not
16 otherwise appropriated for the fiscal year 2026-2027, the sum of three million dollars
17 ($3,000,000) to Rhode Island Legal Services, Inc.; and the state controller is hereby directed to
18 draw their orders upon the general treasurer for the payment of said sum, or so much thereof

1 as may be from time to time required, upon receipt of properly authenticated vouchers.
