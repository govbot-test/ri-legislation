S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION OF $3,000,000 TO THE RHODE ISLAND FOOD BANK
Introduced By: Representatives Slater, Diaz, Sanchez, and Morales
Date Introduced: January 21, 2026
Referred To: House Finance
1 WHEREAS, The demand for emergency food assistance reached historic highs in Rhode
2 Island in 2025, with over 102,000 Rhode Islanders receiving assistance from an area food pantry
3 in November of 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, One in three Rhode Island households are facing food insecurity; and
5 WHEREAS, Almost 32,000 Rhode Islanders will see their SNAP benefits reduced or
6 stopped altogether due to federal SNAP changes from Congress’s passage of H.R.1 legislation in
7 July of 2025; and
8 WHEREAS, The loss of SNAP benefits to Rhode Islanders will equal $9.5 million
9 dollars; and
10 WHEREAS, Individuals who lose SNAP or have reduced benefits will need to rely more
11 on area food pantries; and
12 WHEREAS, The Rhode Island Community Food Bank provides food and support to 137
13 member agencies in every area of Rhode Island and has seen their federal support for food
14 already reduced; now, therefore be it
15 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated, out of money in the treasury not
16 otherwise appropriated for the fiscal year 2026-2027, the sum of three million dollars
17 ($3,000,000) to the Rhode Island Food Bank; and the state controller is hereby authorized and
18 directed to draw their orders upon the general treasurer for the payment of said sum, or so much

1 thereof as may be from time to time required, upon receipt of properly authenticated vouchers.
