It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 31-8-1.1 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-8 entitled "Offenses
2 Against Registration and Certificate of Title Laws" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 31-8-1.1. Operation of beach vehicle without permit.
4 (a) No person shall operate, nor shall an owner knowingly permit to be operated, upon a
5 beach as defined by the coastal resources management council, any vehicle required to be registered
6 by the coastal resources management council unless there has been issued a valid beach registration
7 permit and unless there is attached on it and displayed on it a valid registration sticker issued by
8 the coastal resources management council for the current registration year. Any violation of this
9 section shall be punishable by a fine of not less that twenty-five dollars ($25.00) nor more than
10 seventy-five dollars ($75.00).
11 (b) Violations of this section are subject to fines enumerated in § 31-41.1-4.
12 SECTION 2. Sections 31-19-5, 31-19-6 and 31-19-19 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-
13 19 entitled "Operation of Bicycles" are hereby amended to read as follows:
14 31-19-5. Clinging to vehicles.
15 (a) No person riding upon any bicycle, coaster, roller skates, sled, or toy vehicle shall attach
16 it or himself or herself to any vehicle upon a roadway.
17 (b) This section shall not prohibit attaching a trailer or semi-trailer to a bicycle if that trailer
18 or semi-trailer has been designed for this attachment.

1 (c) Violations of this section are subject to fines enumerated in § 31-41.1-4.
2 31-19-6. Bicycles to right of road.
3 (a) Every person operating a bicycle upon a roadway shall ride as near to the right side of
4 the roadway as practicable, exercising due care when passing a standing vehicle or one proceeding
5 in the same direction except where official traffic control devices (signs or pavement markings)
6 specifically direct bicyclists to do otherwise.
7 (b) Violations of this section are subject to fines enumerated in § 31-4.1-4.
8 31-19-19. Use of coasters, roller skates, and similar devices restricted.
9 (a) No person upon roller skates, or riding in or by means of any coaster, toy vehicle, or
10 similar device, shall go upon any roadway except while crossing a street on a crosswalk, and when
11 so crossing, the person shall be granted all of the rights and shall be subject to all of the duties
12 applicable to pedestrians. This section shall not apply upon any street while set aside as a play street
13 by a duly authorized police authority.
14 (b) Violations of this section are subject to fines enumerated in § 31-41.1-4.
