S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- ORDINANCES
Introduced By: Senators Britto, and Lawson
Date Introduced: May 26, 2026
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-6 of the General Laws entitled "Ordinances" is hereby amended
2 by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-6-13. Hotel, motel, inn, boardinghouse, or catering establishment – Application;
4 approval.
5 The city of East Providence, is hereby authorized and empowered to provide, by ordinance,
6 for the discretionary issuance and revocation of licenses to any person or business entity seeking to
7 engage in the business of operating a hotel, motel, inn, boardinghouse, or catering establishment,
8 to establish reasonable fees for the issuance and revocation, and to promulgate, by ordinance,
9 reasonable rules and regulations as to the duration, location, number, and operation of those licenses
10 within the city. Prior to issuance of a license by the city clerk, pursuant to this section, any person
11 or business entity seeking to engage in the business of operating a hotel, motel, inn, boardinghouse,
12 or catering establishment shall first obtain approval of the chief of police, the building inspector
13 and the mayor.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- ORDINANCES
***
1 This act would authorize the city of East Providence to issue licenses to any person or
2 business entity seeking to engage in the business of operating a hotel, motel, inn, boardinghouse,
3 or catering establishment. The act would also provide that the business in question first obtains
4 approval from the chief of police, the building inspector and the mayor.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
