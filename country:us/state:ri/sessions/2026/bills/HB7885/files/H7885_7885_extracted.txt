S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES AND CARRIERS -- REGULATORY POWERS OF
ADMINISTRATION
Introduced By: Representatives Sanchez, Morales, Fellela, and Serpa
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 39-3-37.3 of the General Laws in Chapter 39-3 entitled "Regulatory
2 Powers of Administration" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 39-3-37.3. Informational notice on electric bills — Electric distribution company.
4 (a) Every electric distribution company that shall charge for the distribution of electricity
5 to any house, building, tenement, or estate shall conspicuously display upon the bill or statement
6 for any customer the following information:
7 (1) The total number of kilowatt hours consumed;
8 (2) The total cost of distributing the consumer power to the customer;
9 (3) Transition charges;
10 (4) Conservation costs;
11 (5) The total cost of transmitting the consumed power to the appropriate distribution site;
12 (6) All applicable credits;
13 (7) Applicable streetlight rental costs;
14 (8) Applicable taxes;
15 (9) The cost of power delivered; and
16 (10) All other costs, charges, or fees added to the bill or statement.
17 (b) The electric distribution company shall issue a single bill for electric service to all
18 customers in its service territory; provided however, that customers of nonregulated power

1 producers may request that the nonregulated power producers provide separate bills for electricity
2 supply.
3 (c) For purposes of this section, “conspicuously display” means including a visual
4 illustration showing each component of the utility bill and the cost associated with each component
5 including, but not limited to, supply, distribution, transmission, ratepayer funded programs and
6 policy, taxes and all other costs. The division shall have the authority to approve or deny the
7 adequacy of the format chosen by the company for compliance with this provision.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
