S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES
Introduced By: Representatives Craven, Bennett, and O'Brien
Date Introduced: February 12, 2026
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 31-3 of the General Laws entitled "Registration of Vehicles" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 31-3-132. Special plate for the Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame.
4 (a) The administrator of the division of motor vehicles is empowered to make available
5 special motor vehicle registration plates for the not-for-profit entity Rhode Island Hockey Hall of
6 Fame. The plates shall be designed to reference the not-for-profit entity Rhode Island Hockey Hall
7 of Fame.
8 (b) The special plate shall be displayed upon the same registration number assigned to the
9 vehicle for which it was issued and shall be used in place of and in the same manner as the
10 registration plates issued to the vehicle. The original registration plates for the vehicle shall be
11 removed from the vehicle and returned to the division of motor vehicles. The registration certificate
12 for the plates shall be carried in the vehicle in accordance with § 31-3-9. The registration certificate
13 shall be in effect for the special plate.
14 (c) The Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame motor vehicle plates shall be the same size as
15 regular motor vehicle plates and shall be designed by the Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame in
16 conjunction with the division of motor vehicles, with the design approved by the Rhode Island state
17 police.
18 (d) The Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame plates shall be subject to compliance with §
19 31-3-117 relating to the number of minimum prepaid orders of plates per plate type (i.e., passenger,

1 commercial, etc.) and any required payment. The Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame plates shall
2 not be issued unless the minimum order requirements are met. The initial order will be handled by
3 the Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame and shall not be submitted to the division of motor vehicles
4 for production until the minimum order(s) have been met and the proper paper work submitted to
5 the division.
6 (e) The administrator of motor vehicles shall develop prepayment procedures and any other
7 procedures deemed necessary to carry out the purposes of this section.
8 (f) In addition to the regular prescribed motor vehicle registration fee, Rhode Island
9 Hockey Hall of Fame plates shall be subject to a forty dollar ($40.00) issuance surcharge.
10 (g) The forty dollar ($40.00) issuance surcharge shall be allocated as follows: twenty
11 dollars ($20.00) shall be allocated to the general fund and the remaining twenty dollars ($20.00)
12 shall be distributed annually to the Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame to commemorate, preserve,
13 and promote the game of hockey in Rhode Island.
14 (h) A ten dollar ($10.00) surcharge for subsequent registration renewals shall be allocated
15 to the Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame.
16 (i) In consideration of the receipt of funds from the registration of Rhode Island Hockey
17 Hall of Fame plates, the Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame must use any Rhode Island-sourced
18 funds in and for the benefit of Rhode Island-based charitable organizations.
19 (j) The Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame will be required to submit an annual accounting
20 report before such monies are distributed.
21 (k) There shall be no refunds for early cancellation of Rhode Island Hockey Hall of Fame
22 plates.
23 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
