LC006300

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
Introduced By: Representatives Donovan, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Chippendale,
Kennedy, Ajello, Tanzi, Santucci, Cortvriend, and Boylan
Date Introduced: April 16, 2026
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, Family medicine physicians provide comprehensive and patient-centered
2 care to individuals and families across all ages, genders, and conditions; and
3 WHEREAS, These physicians serve as the frontline of our healthcare system, improving
4 health outcomes, reducing costs, and strengthening communities through preventive care and
5 early intervention; and
6 WHEREAS, Family medicine physicians play a critical role in addressing public health
7 challenges, including chronic disease, behavioral health needs, and health disparities; and
8 WHEREAS, Advocacy Day on April 16, 2026, brings together family medicine
9 physicians from across Rhode Island to engage with policymakers and promote policies that
10 expand access to care, support primary care, and improve the health of all Rhode Islanders; and
11 WHEREAS, The State of Rhode Island recognizes the dedication, expertise, and
12 compassionate service of family medicine physicians, as well as their commitment to advancing a
13 more equitable and effective healthcare system; now, therefore be it
14 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
15 honors family medicine physicians on the occasion of their Advocacy Day on April 16, 2026, at
16 the Rhode Island State House and expresses its gratitude for their invaluable contributions to the
17 health and well-being of the people of Rhode Island; and be it further
1 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
2 transmit duly certified copies of this resolution to the Rhode Island Academy of Family
3 Physicians.
