A N A C T
RELATING TO FOOD AND DRUGS -- UNIFORM CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES ACT
Introduced By: Representatives Potter, Speakman, Donovan, Ajello, Giraldo, Slater,
McNamara, Edwards, Batista, and Dawson
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: House Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 21-28 of the General Laws entitled "Uniform Controlled Substances
2 Act" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 21-28-2.11. Exemption of psilocybin.
4 (a) Nothing in this chapter shall apply to any compound, mixture, or preparation containing
5 less than one ounce (1 oz.) of psilocybin provided the following conditions have been met:
6 (1) Psilocybin is in possession of one person or shared by one person to another; or
7 (2) Psilocybin has been securely cultivated within a person’s residence for personal use.
8 (b) Contingent upon the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) rescheduling of psilocybin,
9 the Rhode Island department of health shall establish rules and regulations pertaining to cultivation,
10 distribution and medical prescription.
11 (c) Contingent upon the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) expansion of its access
12 program, patients with a serious or life-threatening mental or behavioral health disorder, who are
13 without access to effective mental or behavioral health medication, shall have access to psilocybin
14 at locations in Rhode Island approved by the Rhode Island department of health.
15 (d) The provisions of this section shall sunset on July 1, 2028, and prior to this sunset, the
16 attorney general shall provide a report to the speaker of the house and president of the senate
17 relating to the number of violations issued for possession, cultivation, or distribution of psilocybin,
18 and the director of the department of health shall provide a report to the speaker of the house and
19 president of the senate relating to the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) scheduling of

1 psilocybin and permitted use for the treatment of mental or behavioral health disorders.
2 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026, and shall sunset on July 1, 2028.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO FOOD AND DRUGS -- UNIFORM CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES ACT
***
1 This act would amend the current law on controlled substances to permit psilocybin to be
2 securely cultivated within a person’s residence for personal use. The act would also, contingent on
3 the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) rescheduling and expanding its access program, mandate
4 that the department of health establish rules and regulations on the cultivation, distribution and
5 medical prescription of psilocybin.
6 This act would take effect on July 1, 2026, and would sunset on July 1, 2028.
