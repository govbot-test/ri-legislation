JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES AND CARRIERS -- PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION
Introduced By: Senators Rogers, Paolino, E Morgan, de la Cruz, and Raptakis
Date Introduced: January 16, 2026
Referred To: Senate Commerce
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 39-1 of the General Laws entitled "Public Utilities Commission" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 39-1-33.1. Report on electrification.
4 The public utilities commission shall produce a report by January 1, 2027 and each January
5 1 thereafter, containing an analysis of the electric grid and its ability and/or capacity to handle the
6 electrification load needed to power cars, buildings, and heating of homes within the state. The
7 report shall be provided to the speaker of the house, the senate president, and the governor.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES AND CARRIERS -- PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION
***
1 This act would require the public utilities commission (PUC) to produce a report by
2 January 1, 2027 and each January 1 thereafter, on the ability of the current electric grid to handle
3 the electrification load needed to power cars, buildings, and heating of homes within the state and
4 provide the report to the speaker of the house, the senate president and the governor.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
