A N A C T
RELATING TO COURTS AND CIVIL PROCEDURE -- PROCEDURE GENERALLY --
CAUSES OF ACTION
Introduced By: Senators Vargas, Britto, DiPalma, Urso, Famiglietti, Acosta, Bell,
Appollonio, and Patalano
Date Introduced: February 27, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 9-1 of the General Laws entitled "Causes of Action" is hereby
2 amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 9-1-55. Recovery of replacement value of a child restraint system in a motor vehicle
4 accident.
5 (a) Any person responsible for a collision with a motor vehicle, which contains or employs
6 a federally approved child restraint system under 49 CFR § 571.213 that is being used to transport
7 a child in compliance with § 31-22-22 or which is unoccupied at the time of the collision, shall be
8 liable to the owner of the child restraint system for the replacement value of the child restraint
9 system.
10 (b) The person responsible for the collision or their insurer shall be entitled to take
11 possession of the child restraint system upon payment of the replacement value pursuant to the
12 provisions of subsection (a) of this section.
13 (c) The provision of this section shall apply regardless of the lack of any visible damage to
14 the child restraint system.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
