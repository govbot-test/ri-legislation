A N A C T
RELATING TO TAXATION -- RHODE ISLAND NEW QUALIFIED JOBS INCENTIVE ACT
2015
Introduced By: Senators Ujifusa, Bell, Euer, Murray, Mack, Acosta, Valverde, DiMario,
Lauria, and DiPalma
Date Introduced: February 13, 2026
Referred To: Senate Labor & Gaming
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 44-48.3-12 of the General Laws in Chapter 44-48.3 entitled "Rhode
2 Island New Qualified Jobs Incentive Act 2015" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 44-48.3-12. Discontinuance of further rate reductions and future beneficiaries under
4 the jobs development act.
5 (a) The rate reduction(s) provided pursuant to chapter 64.5 of title 42 shall be discontinued
6 effective July 1, 2015, except as provided in subsection (b) of this section; provided, however, that
7 all rate reductions provided pursuant to chapter 64.5 of title 42 will discontinue no later than July
8 1, 2026.
9 (b) Any company that has qualified for a rate reduction pursuant to chapter 64.5 of title 42
10 prior to July 1, 2015, shall be entitled to maintain the rate reduction in effect as of June 30, 2015,
11 and no additional rate reduction shall be permitted. All obligations of the company required under
12 chapter 64.5 of title 42 to retain a rate reduction shall remain in full force and effect.
13 SECTION 2. Chapter 42-64.5 of the General Laws entitled "Jobs Development Act" is
14 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
15 42-64.5-9. Sunset.
16 No credits shall be authorized to be reserved pursuant to this chapter after June 30, 2026.

1 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.
