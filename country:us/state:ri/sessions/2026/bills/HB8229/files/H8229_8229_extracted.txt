S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2026
____________
A N A C T
TO VACATE THE FORFEITURE OR REVOCATION OF THE CHARTER OF THE DR.
MARTIN LUTHER KING JR., PARENT-TEACHER ORGANIZATION
Introduced By: Representative Rebecca M. Kislak
Date Introduced: March 04, 2026
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The time within which the Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr., Parent-Teacher
2 Organization incorporated on November 4, 1983 shall file the reports for the years 1983, 1984,
3 1985, 1986, 1987, 1988, 1989, 1990, 1991, 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, 2000,
4 2001, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016,
5 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025 and 2026, as required under the provisions
6 of Chapter 6 of Title 7 of the General Laws, 1956, is hereby extended to the 31st day of December,
7 A.D., 2026, if said corporation shall file in the office of the secretary of state before December 31,
8 2026, the reports which it should have filed in the said years and shall pay to the secretary of state
9 the filing fees therefor, having previously thereto paid to the general treasurer for the use of the
10 state such sum as the attorney general shall decide should be paid by said corporation as a
11 compromise for the penalty or penalties for its failure to file such reports then the forfeiture or
12 revocation of the charter of said corporation shall thereupon, to wit, upon the filing of said reports
13 and the payment of said fees, be vacated and said corporation shall thereupon be entitled to all of
14 the privileges, benefits and powers and subject to the same duties and liabilities as though no
15 forfeiture or revocation of its said charter had taken place.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
