S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING THE REPORTING DATE AND AMENDING THE LANGUAGE OF THE
SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY RENEWABLE ENERGY PROGRAMS
Introduced By: Senator V. Susan Sosnowski
Date Introduced: June 09, 2026
Referred To: Placed on the Senate Consent Calendar
1 RESOLVED, That the special legislative commission created by resolution No. 378
2 passed by the Senate at its January session, A.D. 2024, and approved June 13, 2024, entitled
3 "Senate Resolution Creating a Special Legislative Commission to Study Renewable Energy
4 Programs”, and as extended and amended by resolution No. 141 passed by the Senate at its
5 January session, A.D., 2025, and approved March 11, 2025, entitled “Senate Resolution
6 Extending the Reporting Date and Amending the Language of the Special Legislative
7 Commission to Study Renewable Energy Programs” and as further extended and amended by
8 resolution No. 374 passed by the Senate at its January session, A.D. 2025, and approved June 20,
9 2025, entitled “Senate Resolution Extending the Reporting Date and Amending the Language of
10 the Special Legislative Commission to Study Renewable Energy Programs” is hereby further
11 authorized to continue its study and make a report to the Senate on or before January 31, 2027,
12 and said commission shall expire on February 27, 2027; and be it further
13 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting authorized by resolution No. 374 passed by the
14 Senate at its January session, A.D. 2025, and approved June 20, 2025, be and the same is hereby
15 rescinded.
