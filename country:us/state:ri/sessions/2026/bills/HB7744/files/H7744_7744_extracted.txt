A N A C T
RELATING TO INSURANCE -- ACCIDENT AND SICKNESS INSURANCE POLICIES
Introduced By: Representative Brandon C. Potter
Date Introduced: February 12, 2026
Referred To: House Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 27-18-33 of the General Laws in Chapter 27-18 entitled "Accident
2 and Sickness Insurance Policies" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 27-18-33. Drug coverage.
4 No Any group health insurer subject to the provisions of this chapter that provides coverage
5 for prescription drugs under a group plan master contract delivered, issued for delivery, or renewed
6 in this state shall offer a minimum of two (2) options for the delivery of prescription drugs, but is
7 prohibited from requiring may require any person covered under the contract to obtain prescription
8 drugs from a mail order pharmacy as a condition of obtaining benefits for the drugs.
9 SECTION 2. Section 27-19-26 of the General Laws in Chapter 27-19 entitled "Nonprofit
10 Hospital Service Corporations" is hereby amended to read as follows:
11 27-19-26. Drug coverage.
12 No Any group health insurer subject to the provisions of this chapter that provides coverage
13 for prescription drugs under a group plan master contract delivered, issued for delivery, or renewed
14 in this state shall offer a minimum of two (2) options for the delivery of prescription drugs, but is
15 prohibited from requiring may require any person covered under the contract to obtain prescription
16 drugs from a mail order pharmacy as a condition of obtaining benefits for the drugs.
17 SECTION 3. Section 27-20-23 of the General Laws in Chapter 27-20 entitled "Nonprofit
18 Medical Service Corporations" is hereby amended to read as follows:
19 27-20-23. Drug coverage.

1 No Any group health insurer subject to the provisions of this chapter that provides coverage
2 for prescription drugs under a group plan master contract delivered, issued for delivery, or renewed
3 in this state shall offer a minimum of two (2) options for the delivery of prescription drugs, but is
4 prohibited from requiring may require any person covered under the contract to obtain prescription
5 drugs from a mail order pharmacy as a condition of obtaining benefits for the drugs.
6 SECTION 4. Section 27-41-38 of the General Laws in Chapter 27-41 entitled "Health
7 Maintenance Organizations" is hereby amended to read as follows:
8 27-41-38. Drug coverage.
9 No Any health maintenance organization that provides coverage for prescription drugs
10 under a group plan master contract delivered, issued for delivery, or renewed in this state shall offer
11 a minimum of two (2) options for the delivery of prescription drugs, but is prohibited from requiring
12 may require any person covered under the contract to obtain prescription drugs from a mail order
13 pharmacy as a condition of obtaining benefits for the drugs.
14 SECTION 5. This act shall take effect upon passage.
