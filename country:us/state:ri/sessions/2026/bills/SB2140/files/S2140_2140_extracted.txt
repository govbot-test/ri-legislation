A N A C T
RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS AND GOVERNMENT -- QUASI-PUBLIC
CORPORATIONS ACCOUNTABILITY AND TRANSPARENCY ACT
Introduced By: Senators Bissaillon, LaMountain, Appollonio, McKenney, and
Famiglietti
Date Introduced: January 16, 2026
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 42-155-6 of the General Laws in Chapter 42-155 entitled "Quasi-
2 Public Corporations Accountability and Transparency Act" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 42-155-6. Transparency requirements established.
4 The following shall be public, and available to the public upon request and posted directly,
5 or via direct link, on the website of each quasi-public corporation:
6 (1) Job descriptions of the executive director and management;
7 (2) Compensation comparability studies of the executive and senior management, and
8 current and approved salaries;
9 (3) Quarterly financial statements;
10 (4) Capital improvement plans;
11 (5) Operating budgets;
12 (6) Strategic plan;
13 (7) Agendas and minutes of the open meetings of the board, including all documents to be
14 discussed at an open meeting of a public body shall be posted or linked with the electronic filing
15 of the agenda submitted to the secretary of state pursuant to § 42-46-6;
16 (8) Quarterly contracting reports and annual contracting reports required by § 42-90-1;
17 (9) Regulations adopted by the quasi-public corporation;
18 (10) Enabling legislation;

1 (11) Mission statement;
2 (12) Board members;
3 (13) Organizational chart and list of current salaries of positions;
4 (14) Bylaws of the quasi-public corporation;
5 (15) All reports and audits required by this chapter or other general laws; and
6 (16) Any and all policies and procedures established by the board members pursuant to the
7 provisions of § 42-155-4(b).
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS AND GOVERNMENT -- QUASI-PUBLIC
CORPORATIONS ACCOUNTABILITY AND TRANSPARENCY ACT
***
1 This act would provide that compensation comparability studies of senior management and
2 current salaries, all documents discussed at an open meeting, annual contracting reports, list of
3 current salaries and positions, and any and all policies and procedures of board members of public
4 corporations be made publicly available.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
