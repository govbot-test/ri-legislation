EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO HEALTH AND SAFETY -- THE HOSPITAL CONVERSIONS ACT
***
1 This act would amend the requirements pursuant to the Hospital Conversions Act to
2 authorize the attorney general and the department of health to permit the filing of an initial
3 application for conversion pursuant to the expedited review provisions of § 23-17.14-12.1 for a
4 hospital subject to court-supervised insolvency proceedings. The provisions of this act shall sunset
5 one year from the effective date of the act, except that the sunset provision shall not inhibit review
6 by either department for any application that was filed on or before the sunset date.
7 This act would take effect upon passage.
